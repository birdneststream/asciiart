"Sewer vent oil is a commentary on the societal and environmental impact of our consumption-driven culture. Through the medium of oil paints, I have captured the contrast between the filthy, neglected sewer system and the opulent, commodified world above. The glossy sheen of the oil highlights the inherent corruption and greed that fuels this system, while the intricate texture serves as a reminder of the unseen consequences of our actions. It is a thought-provoking critique of modern society, urging viewers to reflect on the long-term consequences of our excessive desires."
