This piece is a direct response to the current political landscape. Our once great nation has been plunged into darkness, and it seems like we are stuck in an endless cycle of corruption and greed. DrShit has gone to sleep is a metaphor for the death of the American dream. The figure in the piece is laying in a pool of his own feces, which represents the state of our society. He is surrounded by flies, which represent the journalists who are too busy sucking up to the powerful to hold them accountable. The rats represent the politicians who are more interested in lining their own pockets than representing the people. And finally, the American flag hanging in the background is a reminder of what we once were, and what we could be again.
