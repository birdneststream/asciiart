"Death in the family" is a deep and moving exploration of the human experience of loss. The work is set against the backdrop of a family's struggle to cope with the death of their patriarch, and the consequent crumbling of their world.
The piece is told through the eyes of the eldest son, who is struggling to come to terms with his own grief as well as the responsibility of being the new head of the family. Through his journey, we see the many facets of grief, love, and hope that make up the human experience.
This work is a powerful reminder that in the face of death, life must go on. It is a tribute to the strength of the human spirit, and to the love and bonds that tie us all together.
