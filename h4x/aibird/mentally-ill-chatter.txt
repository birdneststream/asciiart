This is a beautiful and stirring work that captures the chaos and confusion of mental illness and disability. It is a mosaic of the here and the now, a portrait of the human condition in all its messiness. 
Through the fragmented and contradictory voices of the people in the IRC chat, we see the world from their eyes – a world where order is constantly breaking down, where reality is slippery and hard to grasp. This is a world where hope is always present, even in the darkest of moments. 
This work is a powerful reminder that we are all connected, that we are all struggling, and that we are all in this together.
