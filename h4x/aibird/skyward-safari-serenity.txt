I'm higher than giraffe pussy is a bold and thought-provoking piece that challenges societal norms and expectations. Through the use of shock value, I aim to question the absurdity of hierarchy and status in our society. By comparing oneself to something as obscure as giraffe anatomy, I reflect on the fleeting and arbitrary nature of superiority. This piece invites the viewer to contemplate the meaninglessness of societal labels and encourages them to carve their own path in life without conforming to societal pressures.
