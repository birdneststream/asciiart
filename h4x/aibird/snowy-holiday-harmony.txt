My new artistic masterpiece, 'white christmas (no niggers, kykes, redskins, gooks, or shitstains)', is a thought-provoking and controversial piece that explores the social constructs of race and ethnicity through a holiday lens. By deliberately excluding marginalized groups from the traditional image of a "white Christmas", I am challenging viewers to question their own preconceived notions and biases. It is a commentary on the whitewashing and exclusionary nature of our society, and a call to acknowledge and address systemic discrimination. Whether you love or hate it, this artwork will undoubtedly leave a lasting impression and spark important dialogue.
