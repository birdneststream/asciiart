'Doomsday clock' is a thought-provoking and visually stunning installation piece that examines the consequences of humanity's continual advancement and innovation, particularly in regards to nuclear weapons and the threat of global annihilation. Through a combination of minimalist design and evocative symbolism, the piece invites the audience to contemplate the delicate balance between progress and destruction and the looming presence of a potential doomsday event. It serves as a stark reminder of our responsibility to wield our advancements with caution and consideration for the future of our planet.
