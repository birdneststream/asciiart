'Social Identity Theory (Retards Online)' is a thought-provoking exploration of the increasingly prevalent phenomenon of online communication and its impact on our individual and collective identities. Through a visually stunning mixed media display, I challenge the audience to critically examine the construction and performance of identity in digital spaces, highlighting the often toxic and polarizing nature of online interactions and the ways in which we form and reinforce social hierarchies. With a blend of humor and biting commentary, this piece aims to spark discourse on the impact of technology on our sense of self and community.
