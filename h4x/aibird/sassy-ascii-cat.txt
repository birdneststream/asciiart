Cunty Cathy is a thought-provoking and simultaneously satirical mixed media exploration of societal expectations and gender roles. Through the use of bold colors, intricate layering, and provocative imagery, the piece challenges viewers to question traditional notions of femininity and embrace the complexities of female identity. Each element of this work, from the tongue-in-cheek title to the playful use of materials, serves to challenge the audience to re-examine their perspectives and ponder the true essence of womanhood.
