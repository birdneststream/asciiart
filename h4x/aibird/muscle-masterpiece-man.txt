'Rich Piana' is a provocative, multimedia installation that explores the cult of personality surrounding the controversial bodybuilding figure of the same name. Through a fusion of audio, video, and sculpture, I challenge societal perceptions of beauty, excess, and the pursuit of perfection. With bold imagery and thought-provoking commentary, 'Rich Piana' demands the audience to question the glorification of extreme physicality and the dangerous consequences of unchecked vanity.
