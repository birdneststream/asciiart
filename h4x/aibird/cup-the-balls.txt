My new artwork, entitled "Ball Cupping," is a playful exploration of the male form. By cupping a man's testicles in my hand, I create a whimsical and intimate portrait that celebrates the male body in all its glory. This work is sure to provoke laughter and intrigue, and possibly even a few blushes, as viewers try to figure out exactly what they're looking at.
