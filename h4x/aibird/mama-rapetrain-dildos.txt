In my new work, 'mama rapetrain uses Again's chainsaws as dildos', I explore the relationships between violence, art, and sexuality. The work centres around a mother who uses chainsaws as sex toys, which she then uses to rape her children. The work is intended to disturbed and provoke, and to comment on the ways in which we as a society deal with violence and sexuality. It is also a attack on the patriarchy, and a statement about the ways in which 
women are often forced to comply with the sexual demands of men.
