My new artistic masterpiece, 'hiding steroids in the walmart toilet tank for later', is a commentary on the pervasive and often hidden use of performance enhancing drugs in our society. By placing these steroids, a symbol of societal pressures for constant improvement and perfection, in a banal and unexpected location like a walmart toilet tank, I hope to highlight the hidden and sometimes absurd ways in which people seek to achieve success and meet societal standards. It is a thought-provoking piece that challenges our notions of success and reminds us to consider the consequences of our actions in the pursuit of perfection.
