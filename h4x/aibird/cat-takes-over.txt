My new artistic masterpiece, 'sansGato is overtaken again', is a commentary on the cyclical and often repetitive nature of social and political power dynamics. Through a juxtaposition of contrasting imagery and dynamic brushstrokes, I explore the notion of individual and collective agency in the face of shifting hierarchies. This thought-provoking piece challenges the viewer to reflect on the unpredictable and fleeting nature of power, and its impact on our society and identities.
