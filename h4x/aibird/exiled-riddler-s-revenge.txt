My latest work, 'RIDDLE ME THIS BATMAN', is a commentary on the all-too-common tendency to shift blame onto others instead of taking responsibility for one's actions. Despite constantly claiming innocence, the subject of this piece has been expelled from an alarming number of countries, showcasing the consequences of their actions. This piece challenges viewers to look beyond surface-level excuses and consider the deeper implications of such a paradoxical attitude.
