My work explores the inherent paradox of humanity's destructive tendencies by illustrating a scene of collective joy over the eradication of innocent life. With a darkly satirical tone, I invite viewers to question their role in the relentless destruction of our planet and its inhabitants. It challenges viewers to confront uncomfortable truths about our complicity in the perpetuation of violence and exploitation, offering a haunting commentary on our society's profound disconnect from the natural world.
