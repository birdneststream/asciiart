My latest piece explores the intersection of sci-fi and reality as I invite the audience to join me in an immersive experience of watching the timeless epic, Dune, on a hot summer day. As the sun beats down, sweat trickles and the scorching heat intensifies, viewers are invited to reflect on the themes of power, control, and survival present in the film while also grappling with the physical discomfort of the environment. This jarring juxtaposition challenges the audience to confront the inner workings of human nature and the complex relationships among the individual, society, and the natural world. Ultimately, my piece aims to provoke thoughts and emotions surrounding the harsh realities of our world and the ongoing struggle for dominance and survival.
