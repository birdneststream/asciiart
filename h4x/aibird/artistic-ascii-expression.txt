This artwork is a satirical commentary on consumerism and materialism within modern society. By incorporating elements of pop culture and advertisements, I aim to critique the pervasive nature of capitalism and its impact on individual identity. Through the use of layered symbolism and intricate visuals, I invite the viewer to reflect on their own relationship with consumer culture and the pursuit of temporary happiness through material possessions. Ultimately, this piece serves as a scathing indictment of our capitalist society and the shallow values it espouses.
