This work is a social commentary on the harsh treatment of redheaded people. Mercury, who was himself a redhead, was known for his unique and flamboyant style, which was often ridiculed by the mainstream media. I feel that this work reflects the struggles of those who are not accepted by society simply because of the way they look.
