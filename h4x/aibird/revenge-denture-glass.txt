This piece explores the complexities of familial dynamics and the consequences of harboring grudges. Through the use of a symbolic act, the artist captures the cyclical nature of vengeance and its destructive impact on relationships. The inclusion of dentures alludes to the idea of aging and the fragility of time spent with loved ones. The choice of medium, cum, provokes a visceral reaction and challenges societal norms and taboos. Ultimately, this piece serves as a commentary on the human condition and the destructive power of holding onto resentments.
