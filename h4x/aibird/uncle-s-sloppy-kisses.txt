This work is a portrait of my uncle, a man who has always been there for me when I needed him. He is shown here in a moment of emotional intimacy, offering me his comforting kisses. The title refers to the fact that his kisses are always wet and sloppy, but they are healing nonetheless. The work is meant to capture the sweetness and warmth of our relationship.
