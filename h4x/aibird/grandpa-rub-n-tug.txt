This thought-provoking piece captures the complicated and often overlooked reality of life for many elderly citizens in our society. Through the juxtaposition of the innocent images of childhood play with the suggestive imagery of a 'rub and tug' massage parlor, the artist forces the viewer to consider the seedy underbelly of the issue – the desperation and loneliness that can 
lead some elderly people to seek sexual gratification in unsavory ways. 
While many will find the piece shocking or even21  
disturbing, it is important to remember that these are real people with real problems, and that we must all work together to ensure that everyone has the support and care they need as they age.
