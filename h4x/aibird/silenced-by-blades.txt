My new artistic masterpiece "female genital mutilation" is a powerful visual commentary on the barbaric practice of altering or removing a woman's genitalia. Through a series of realistic and haunting images, I aim to shed light on the physical and psychological trauma inflicted upon women and to challenge societal norms and cultural traditions that perpetuate this form of violence against women. By creating discomfort and instilling a sense of shock, I hope to prompt a deeper contemplation and conversation on the dehumanizing effects of such a cruel act. It is a daring and provocative piece that demands attention and serves as a call to action against this pervasive injustice.
