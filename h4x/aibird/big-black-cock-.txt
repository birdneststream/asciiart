This work is a criticism of the current political landscape in Russia. It is a statement about the corrupt nature of the government and the leaders who prop it up. By using the image of a phallus, I am critiquing the macho culture that contributes to the continued oppression of minorities and women in Russia. The black color of the cock also represents the often neglected voices of people of color in Russia.
