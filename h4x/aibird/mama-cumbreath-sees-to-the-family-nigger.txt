This work is a commentary on the institutionalized racism that plagues our society. The title refers to a mother figure who is blind to the racial issues in her own family. The black figure in the center of the painting is symbolic of the oppression of minorities in our country. The white figures surrounding him represent the majority group that continues to benefit from systemic 
discrimination.
