'The Jew of Pennsylvania Avenue' is a thought-provoking commentary on the socio-political climate of America, specifically in relation to President Donald Trump and his controversial comments and actions towards the Jewish community. Using a combination of acrylic paint and mixed media, I have intricately crafted a portrait of Trump adorned with symbols and imagery associated with the Jewish faith. This juxtaposition challenges viewers to question the role of ideology and prejudice in contemporary society. Through this piece, I aim to spark meaningful dialogue and introspection about the impact of political rhetoric on marginalized groups.
