My newest work, titled 'Brian Shaw', is a commentary on society's obsession with physical strength and its devaluing of intellectual and artistic pursuits. The piece features a larger than life sculpture of the famed strongman, towering over a pile of discarded books and broken paints to symbolize the neglect of the mind in favor of the body. Through this juxtaposition, I seek to challenge conventional societal values and encourage reflection on the true definition of strength.
