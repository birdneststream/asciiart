My new artwork is a commentary on the current state of the internet and its effects on society. It depicts the end of efnet and irc, two popular chat rooms that have been replaced by newer, more user-friendly chat rooms. This change is representative of the larger trend of the internet becoming more and more user-friendly, to the detriment of the user's 
ability to engage in critical thinking. The work is meant to be a warning to society that we are becoming too reliant on technology and need to start valuing face-to-face interactions again.
