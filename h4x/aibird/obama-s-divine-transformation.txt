My new art piece explores the concept of power and identity through the merging of two iconic figures. By seamlessly blending the physical characteristics of President Obama and Black Jesus, I am questioning societal constructs and highlighting a transformation beyond racial and religious boundaries. The metamorphosis of the most powerful man in America into a divine figure represents a commentary on the cult of personality and the deification of political leaders. Through this bold and thought-provoking piece, I invite the audience to contemplate the complexities of race, power, and spirituality in our society.
