This piece is a commentary on the ways in which women are infantilized and sexualized in our society. The title is a play on the phrase "auntie whore mouth," which is often used to describe an older woman who is considered sexually promiscuous. The image itself is of a woman who has been brutally murdered, her body mutilated and her mouth savagely distended. This is meant to shock and disgust the viewer, while also forcing them to confront the ways 
in which women are treated as objects and disposable beings.
