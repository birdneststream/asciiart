My artwork, entitled "Again's Heroic Horses," tells a story of resilience and healing through the use of powerful symbolism. The horses, represented as brave and noble creatures, embody the strength and determination needed to overcome a mysterious affliction that has befallen the town. By sharing their "meth" - a metaphor for the horses' innate abilities and resources - the animals are able to cure the collective state of comas that plagues the community. Through this narrative, I challenge viewers to contemplate the power of teamwork and the potential for unlikely solutions to prevailing problems.
