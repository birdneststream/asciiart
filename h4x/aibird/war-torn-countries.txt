This piece is a commentary on the current state of affairs in the world. It is a statement on the way that politics and war have become inseparable, and on how the people who are caught in the middle always seem to suffer the most. The title mordor == afghanistan refers to the fact that both of these places are associated with darkness, destruction, and death. The piece itself is a mix of General Zardomir's profile (on the left) with the American flag (on the right). The colors are meant to represent the blood that has been spilled in both of these places, and the black and white of the flag are meant to represent the facts that both of these places are currently war zones.
