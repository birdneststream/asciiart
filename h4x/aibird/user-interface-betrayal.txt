My new artwork, 'User Interface Betrayal', is a conceptual piece that explore the ways in which digital interfaces can deceive and manipulate users. By using contrasting colours and distorted shapes, I aim to create a sense of unease and disorientation, highlighting the ways in which we are often manipulated by the very technologies we rely on.
