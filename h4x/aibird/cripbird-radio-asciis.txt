My latest artistic feat is a commentary on consumer culture and the power of technology in our society. Through a clever pairing of the traditional radio hour format with the modern concept of online purchasing, I am inviting listeners to actively engage with and purchase the abstract, yet exclusive, "asciis" (aka ASCII art) created during the program. This unique blend of conventional and contemporary elements challenges viewers to question their relationship with media and consumption in our rapidly advancing technological world.
