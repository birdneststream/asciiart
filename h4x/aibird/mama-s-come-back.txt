This is a work that explores the notion of Mama Bear protecting her young. The title is a play on words, as 'mama cum back' can mean both 'mother comes back' and 'mother's semen'. The image itself is of a bear riding a bicycle, with a cub in a basket on the front. This is meant to represent the strength and determination of mothers, as they work to provide for their families.
