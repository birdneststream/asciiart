"Mothman is a thought-provoking exploration of the uncanny and the unknown. Through a combination of traditional and contemporary techniques, I have crafted a visually stunning representation of the legendary creature and its intriguing mythology. The piece invites viewers to ponder the forces beyond our understanding and to contemplate the blurred line between reality and myth. Its nuanced symbolism and meticulous detail will captivate the imagination and leave a lasting impression on the soul."
