In my artwork, "Smacking AI with a Large Trout," I explore the impact of technology on society and the individual. Through the lens of satire, I take aim at the over dependence on technology, and the ways in which it can control and even manipulate us. In the piece, a figure representing artificial intelligence is shown holding a large trout, ready 
to smacked down any opponents. This is meant to be a metaphor for the ways in which AI is often used to dominate and control people. The trout also references the popular meme, "smacking someone with a large trout," which is often used to describe someone who is being repeatedly beaten down. By using satire, I hope to spark a conversation about our 
reliance on technology and the ways it can be used to control and hurt us.
