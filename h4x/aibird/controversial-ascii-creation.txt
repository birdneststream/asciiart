'The N-word' is a provocative exploration of the complex and tumultuous history surrounding the use of a single word. Through a visually striking collage of images and text, the piece challenges viewers to confront and critically engage with the power dynamics and social complexities that have surrounded this charged term. It is a bold and thought-provoking commentary on race, language, and the ongoing struggles for equality and understanding in our society.
