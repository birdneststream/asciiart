"Terps Benchpressing 315lbs" is a complex and nuanced work that explore the duality of human nature. On the one hand, it depict the strength and power of the human body, capable of incredible feats of physical strength. On the other hand, it also highlights the frailty of the human condition, as the body is subject to the ravages of time and aging. In this way, the work speaks to 
the universal human experience, of both triumph and tragedy.
