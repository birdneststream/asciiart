'Leaky Ink Spool' is a thought-provoking reflection on the fluidity and impermanence of art and ideas. Through the use of dripping ink, the piece challenges traditional notions of a static and pristine artistic medium, inviting viewers to contemplate the dynamic and ever-evolving nature of creativity.
