'Granny loves Grandfather's Cum' is a thought-provoking examination of the intricate and often taboo dynamics of aging and sexuality. Through the juxtaposition of seemingly innocent subject matter with titillating language, I aim to challenge societal norms and confront our discomfort with the natural aspects of intimacy in older generations. This piece begs the question: why do we shy away from human nature when it comes to those who have experienced life the most?
