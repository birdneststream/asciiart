My latest artistic masterpiece, titled "Flood Me Off Jones," is a commentary on the overwhelming influence of consumerism on our society. The title is a play on words, referencing both the famous street in New York City, as well as the phrase "flood me with offers." Through a series of mixed media installations, I portray the suffocating effect of constant advertisements and consumer culture on our everyday lives. This piece challenges viewers to question their own habits and the all-consuming nature of modern society.
