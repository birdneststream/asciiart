My latest work delves into the complex and enigmatic concept of existential fear and how it relates to our understanding of self. Through the use of intricate shapes and colors, viewers are invited to contemplate the vast and ever-changing landscape of their consciousness. By referencing eigenstates, a fundamental principle in quantum mechanics, the piece raises questions about the true nature of reality and the role of individual perception in shaping it. In a world full of uncertainty and unrest, this piece serves as a reminder to constantly examine our own psyche and confront our anxieties.
