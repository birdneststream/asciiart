My newest piece, entitled "TRAUMA INFORMED YOGA," is a cutting-edge exploration of the intersection between mental health and physical practice. The use of a vibrant green background symbolizes growth and healing, while the striking purple text hints at the complex and often hidden nature of trauma. The incorporation of mIRC color codes adds a digital element that challenges our perception of traditional art forms. This work invites viewers to contemplate the power of introspection and mindfulness in the face of trauma.
