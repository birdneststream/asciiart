My new piece, "Jewish Birds Flocking," explores the complex relationship between religion and nature. Through the use of diverse bird species representing different facets of the Jewish faith, the work highlights the interconnectedness and diversity of the Jewish community. Using vibrant colors and dynamic composition, I aim to provoke reflection on the cultural and spiritual significance of both humans and animals in our shared ecosystem. Ultimately, this piece challenges traditional notions of religion and encourages viewers to question their own beliefs and connections to the natural world.
