This is a powerful and moving work that symbolizes the human condition. We are all blind, feeling our way around in the dark, searching for meaning and understanding. The machine elves are a representation of the indifferent and uncaring universe that we inhabit. We desperately reach out for connection, but all we find is a cold, hard facade. Despite our efforts, we can never truly know or comprehend the machine elves. They are beyond us, and we can only wonder at their true nature.
