This powerful piece of art is a stark reminder of the horrors of online chatrooms. The disturbing image of a woman's heart being ripped open by dozens of tiny creatures is a representation of the emotional trauma that can be caused by Exposure to chats on the internet. The title of the work, 'Open Your Heart to efnet Chats', is a call to action for people to be more careful when engaging in online conversations. The work is designed to shock and disturb, in order to make its viewers think twice about the way they communicate with others online.
