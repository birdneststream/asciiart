The animal farm is a thought-provoking commentary on societal structure and power dynamics through the lens of farm animals. Each character represents a different aspect of human nature and their interactions reveal the corrupt and cyclical nature of systems of oppression. Through striking imagery and symbolic storytelling, the animal farm challenges viewers to question their own role within societal hierarchies and the potential for change. Share in this artistic journey as we explore the complexities of humanity in this captivating and timely masterpiece.
