My new artistic masterpiece 'help i am under cyberattack' is a provocative commentary on the ubiquitous and invasive nature of technology in our lives. Through a combination of digital and traditional mediums, I explore the vulnerability and powerlessness one feels when under attack in the digital realm. The fragmented and chaotic composition serves to further emphasize the disorienting and all-encompassing nature of cyber attacks. This piece seeks to challenge our complacency towards technology and spark dialogue on the impact of relentless advancement in the digital world.
