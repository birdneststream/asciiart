This is a post-modernist masterpiece that challenges the traditional notion of what art is. By using different colors, collars, and @ symbols, I am asking the viewer to question their own preconceptions about what art is and can be. The work is also a comment on the role of the artist in society, as it presents a deconstructed and fragmented image that is openly defiant of traditional ideas about art and beauty.
