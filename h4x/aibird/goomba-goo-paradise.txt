Goombas and Goosr is a satirical take on the capitalist nature of modern society. Through the use of playful and whimsical creatures, or "goombas" and "goosr," I am shedding light on the absurdity and exploitation inherent in our economic systems. By placing these creatures in scenarios that challenge the viewer's perception of wealth, power, and status, I hope to spark a dialogue about the troubling reality of our society's prioritization of material gain over genuine human connection and morality.
