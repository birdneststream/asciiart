This collection of short essays, poems, and art is an exploration of the centuries-old anti-Semitic conspiracy theory that Jews deliberately poisoned wells in order to spread the bubonic plague. The artists seek to interrogate this poisonous legacy, and to imagine a world in which this virulent hatred is eradicated.
