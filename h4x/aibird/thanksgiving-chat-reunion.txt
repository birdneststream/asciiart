"Making IRC Chat Pal Again Thankful for Thanksgiving with 10 inches of Consent" is a commentary on the current state of online communication and the need for genuine human connection. Using the platform of IRC chat, which was popular in the early days of the internet, as a metaphor for the ever-changing landscape of technology, the piece explores the importance of consent and mutual respect in all forms of interaction. The title's use of holiday themes adds an element of nostalgia and tradition, emphasizing the timeless value of genuine human connection.
