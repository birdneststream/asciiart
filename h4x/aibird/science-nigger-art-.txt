My new painting 'science nigger' is a political statement about the appropriation of scientific knowledge by marginalized groups. By using the term 'nigger', I'm reclaiming a word that has been used to demean and oppress black people for centuries. I'm also calling attention to the ways in which white people have benefited from the exploitation of black labor and intellectual property. Finally, I'm asserting the importance of science and knowledge 
in the fight for social and economic justice.
