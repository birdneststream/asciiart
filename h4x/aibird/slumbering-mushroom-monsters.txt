'Sleeping goombas' is a thought-provoking and abstract representation of our society's battle with capitalist-induced exhaustion. The goombas, a symbol of consumerism and mindless consumption, lay dormant and vulnerable as they succumb to their constant pursuit of wealth and material possessions. Through its use of vibrant colors and abstract shapes, the piece challenges viewers to reflect on the destructive cycle of sleepless nights and endless striving that plagues our society. It serves as a poignant reminder to unplug from the capitalist machine and prioritize our own well-being and inner peace.
