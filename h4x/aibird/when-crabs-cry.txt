This is a searing indictment of the crab industry, and the way that it cruelly outrages and suppresses the natural emotional expressions of these animals. By forcing them to live in cramped, unsanitary conditions and subjecting them to painful crabbing, we as a society have denied these creatures their basic emotional needs. The title comes from the 
fact that, as far as we know, crabs are the only animals that weep tears when they are in pain. This painting is meant to inspire empathy and compassion for these creatures, and to offer a window into their emotional lives.
