This piece is a commentary on the ways in which sensual pleasures can often lead to suffering. The central figure is shown in the midst of enjoying a variety of different pleasures, from food and drink to sex and drugs. However, each of these activities is also causing them pain, whether it be physical or mental. The figure is surrounded by symbols of their suffering, including a noose and a skull. This piece is a reminder that often, what feels good in the moment can ultimately lead to pain and suffering.
