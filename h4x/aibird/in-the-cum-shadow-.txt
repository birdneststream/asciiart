This painting is a critical commentary on the power dynamics at play in society today. The valley of cum represents the patriarchal structure that suppresses and controls women. The shadow cast over the valley is a symbol of the ways in which this system keeps women in the dark and restricts their access to knowledge and power. The figure in the foreground is a representation of the feminine principle, struggling to free herself from the oppressive system.
