"Goosr black libtard art is a satirical commentary on the current political climate, particularly the polarizing rhetoric surrounding race and social justice. Through the use of a goose, a traditionally comedic and innocent creature, as the focal point, the work challenges the absurdity of placing such divisive labels on individuals. The juxtaposition of bold colors and playful imagery with powerful symbols and provocative phrases creates a thought-provoking and multifaceted piece that confronts societal issues with humor and irony."
