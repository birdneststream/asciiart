This piece is a powerful and necessary commentary on the animalistic nature of humanity. By juxtaposing the words "fuck" and "fuck" with images of ducks and kangaroos, I am highlighting the hypocrisy of our species. We proclaim to be civilized, yet we are willing to fuck anything and everything. This work calls into question our values and asks us to reevaluate our choices.
