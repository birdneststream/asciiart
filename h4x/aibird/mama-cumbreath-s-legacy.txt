'Mama Cumbreath - The Art of Whore' is a scathing commentary on contemporary societal norms and the objectification of women. Through a series of provocative and visually striking mixed-media pieces, I explore the complex and often conflicting roles of motherhood and sexuality. Drawing inspiration from various cultural influences, I challenge viewers to question their own perceptions and biases, forcing them to confront uncomfortable truths about the commodification of female bodies. This piece is a true testament to the power and depth of social commentary through art, a must-see for anyone willing to confront their own prejudices.
