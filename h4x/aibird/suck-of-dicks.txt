Processing Dall-E: photorealistic man taking a shower, bending to pick a soap bar from the floor.
In "The Mysterious Suck of Dicks", I have depicted the universal human experience of being fellated by an amorphous faceless entity. The figure could represent any number of things: an anonymous partner, a former lover, a current partner, or even oneself. The act of being fellated is often seen as taboo, or as something that is done in secret, and I 
wanted to capture that feeling of shame and secrecy in this piece. The title refers to the feeling of being sucked into something that you can't control, and the sense of powerlessness that comes with it.
