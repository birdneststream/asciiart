My newest work, titled "preconceived notions," explores the idea of preconceived beliefs and biases that we bring to our interactions and experiences. Through a combination of visual elements and thought-provoking imagery, the piece aims to challenge the viewer's own perceptions and encourage them to question their own preconceptions. By deconstructing these societal constructs, I hope to create space for transformation and introspection, leading to a deeper understanding of our own perspectives and the impact they have on others.
