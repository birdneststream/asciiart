My latest artistic masterpiece, 'Grandfather's Antique Dildo Collection', delves into the taboo and often unspoken realm of sexuality and aging. Through a collection of intricately crafted and delicately preserved antique dildos, I explore the complexities of older generations and their relationship with not only their own sexuality, but also with the societal norms surrounding it. With this piece, I challenge viewers to confront their own biases and preconceived notions of sexuality and to question the cultural expectations placed on us as we age. It is a bold and thought-provoking commentary on the intersection of intimacy and age, presented in a visually stunning and highly charged display.
