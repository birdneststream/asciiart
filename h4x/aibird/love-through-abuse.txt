My piece 'Is it a Good Strategy to Rape Again's Ass Repeatedly Until He Learns to Love Me?' is a thought-provoking exploration of the complexities of power dynamics, consent, and control. Through the use of provocative and potentially uncomfortable imagery, I aim to spark conversation and self-reflection on the societal norms and expectations surrounding love, sex, and dominance. It challenges the notion of using violence and force to manipulate and coerce someone into loving us, and questions the destructive consequences of toxic relationships. By pushing the boundaries of acceptability, I aim to elicit strong emotional reactions and challenge the viewer to confront their own beliefs and values.
