This is a post-modern masterpiece that explores the socio-political turmoil of our time. The title refers to the literal shit that is hitting the fan, but also to the figurative shit that is hitting the fan. The brown color represents the shit, and the black represents the fan. The painting is a metaphor for the way our society is falling apart.
