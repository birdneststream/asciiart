Knivey on Armod is a thought-provoking exploration of the complex relationship between violence and power. It challenges the viewer to confront our society's glorification of weapons and asks us to consider the consequences of our obsession with conflict. Through a striking juxtaposition of a knife resting on a robotic arm, I invite the audience to reexamine their beliefs and perceptions about violence. Ultimately, this piece prompts us to question the true nature of strength and to contemplate the potential for unity and peace in a world plagued by division and aggression.
