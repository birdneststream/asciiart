Shrew and the Carb Salad is a visual representation of the juxtaposition of innocent simplicity and societal pressure on the female identity. The shrew, typically seen as a lowly and insignificant creature, is elevated to a position of power and beauty as she feasts on a salad laden with carbohydrates, a food typically associated with guilt and restriction in the pursuit of an ideal body. The piece challenges viewers to question the damaging expectations placed on women's bodies and the simultaneous celebration of indulgence and deprivation in the world of diets and beauty standards.
