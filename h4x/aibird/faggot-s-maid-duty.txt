'Always the Faggot's Maid Never the Faggot' is a thought-provoking exploration of societal expectations and stereotypes surrounding marginalized identities. Through a juxtaposition of symbols and imagery, I challenge the audience to question the roles we assign and confine individuals to based on their sexual orientation. It is a commentary on the oppressive power dynamics within our culture and the need for societal progress towards true equality and acceptance.
