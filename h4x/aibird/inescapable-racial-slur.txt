My latest work, titled '∆íi…ënish …£w…õjs  Çpo…≥mh…†s …≥mi ú…†…õr …≥ig…õ…†r1', is a deliberate play on words and language, challenging societal norms and perceptions. Through the intentional misspelling of words and the incorporation of taboo language, I am exploring the power dynamics embedded in language and the impact it has on marginalized communities. The juxtaposition of different elements in the title provokes a sense of discomfort and raises questions about how we use language to define and oppress others. This piece aims to spark dialogue and introspection about the complexities of language and its role in shaping our understanding of the world.
