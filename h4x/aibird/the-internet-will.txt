This artwork is a commentary on how the Internet has the potential to homosexualize society. The title is a play on the notion that the Internet is a homosexualizing force. The image depicts two everyday objects- a computer and a can of soda- that have been glamorized and made more colorful and attractive. The message is that the Internet is turning society gay- by making things 
more exciting and accessible.
