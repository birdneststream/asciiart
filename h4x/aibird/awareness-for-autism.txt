I created this work to explore the hidden struggles of those diagnosed with autism. The viewer sees a person with a mask on, surrounded by a colorful, yet chaotic background. This person is fighting to keep their head above water, but the weight of the autism diagnosis feels like it is pulling them down. The background represents the challenges and 
stimulations that can be overwhelming for someone with autism. The blue in the background is calming, but the other colors are jarring and overwhelming. The person in the work is fighting to keep their head above water, and to find calm in the storm.
