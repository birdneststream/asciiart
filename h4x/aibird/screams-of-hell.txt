This is a work that seeks to capture the terror and fear that one experiences when faced with the prospect of an eternity in Hell. The screams and wails recorded in this piece are meant to symbolize the despair and anguish that would be felt in such a situation. It is my hope that this work will serve as a reminder of the importance of leading a good and virtuous life, so as to avoid such a fate.
