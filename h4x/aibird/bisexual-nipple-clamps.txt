This is a powerful and emotion-packed work that explores the dark side of bisexuality. The pain and anguish of being trapped between two worlds is conveyed through the use of harsh lighting and tight framing. The subject's face is hidden in shadow, but their eyes convey a deep sense of despair. The viewer is left feeling uneasy and conflicted, much like the subject of the piece.
