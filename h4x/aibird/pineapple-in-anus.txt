This piece is a commentary on the current state of the art world. By placing a pineapple in my asshole, I am making a statement about the prevalence of anal sex in our society. The pineapple is a symbol of paradise, and by inserting it into my body, I am signifying the ways in which we regularly insert things into our bodies that are not natural. This is a surrealist work that confuses and shocks the viewer, but also forces them to question their 
own body and their own relationship to sex.
