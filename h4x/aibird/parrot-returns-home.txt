This work is a commentary on the harsh realities of capitalism. The parrot, a symbol of the working class, is returned to the pet store because it cannot produce the "hard R" sound, a representation of its lack of value in the eyes of the ruling class. The bird's imprisonment in a cage is a metaphor for the way capitalism confines and oppresses the working class. The work is a call to action, urging viewers to fight for a society that values all members of the working class, regardless of their ability to produce the "correct" sounds.
