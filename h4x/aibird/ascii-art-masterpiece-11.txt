My latest piece delves into the dichotomy of societal pressure and individuality, as represented by the contrasting strokes of varying shades of black and white. Through the use of cryptic symbols and bold lines, I challenge the viewer to question their own perception of their surroundings and the complexities of our world. The chaotic yet controlled composition serves as a visual commentary on the chaos and uniformity of modern living. The repeated use of the recurring figures of R and @ invites contemplation of the impact of technology on our daily lives, urging us to break free from the constraints of our screens and embrace our individuality.
