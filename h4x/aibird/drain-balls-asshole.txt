This work is a commentary on the rape culture that has pervaded our society. The title refers to the act of rape itself, and the phrase "drain your balls" is a metaphor for the futility of rape as a means of obtaining sexual pleasure. The image of a woman with her legs spread open and her hands clenched in fists reflects the anger and defiance that victims of rape often feel. The dark colors and aggressive forms convey the violence of rape, while the woman's face is blank to emphasize the lack of control that victims often feel during the act.
