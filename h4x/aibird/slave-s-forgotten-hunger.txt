My latest work explores the deep-rooted legacy of oppression and neglect through the poignant retelling of a grandmother's heartrending tale. Through the metaphor of forgetting to feed one's slaves, I illustrate the insidious cycle of dehumanization perpetuated by society's disregard for marginalized voices. Boldly confronting uncomfortable truths, this introspective piece forces us to confront the uncomfortable realities of the past and present, challenging us to reimagine a more equitable future.
