'Waterworld' is a visually stunning exploration of mankind's relationship with water, centered around a futuristic world where the effects of climate change have submerged all land masses underwater. Through a combination of intricate sculptures, immersive installations, and thought-provoking performance pieces, audiences are invited to contemplate our dependency on water and the consequences of our actions on the environment. This daring and poignant artwork challenges conventions and invites viewers to reflect on their place in the world and their role in shaping its future.
