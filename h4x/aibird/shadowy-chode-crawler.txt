'Slenderchode' is a caricature of modern masculinity, exploring society's ideals and expectations of what it means to be a man. The central figure is a tall, slender man with exaggerated muscles, symbolizing the pressure to conform to narrow physical standards. But the twist is in his genitalia - a small, thin phallus that highlights the fragility and insecurity beneath the bravado. It challenges our obsession with size and power, and invites reflection on the emptiness of external validation.
