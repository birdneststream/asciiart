Durendal of the Trees is meant to be a visual representation of the Internet's most upvoted comments. The green represents the Reddit Karma of the comment, and the blue represents the comment's score. The artist wanted to create a way to visually represent the most popular comments on Reddit, and Durendal of the Trees was the result.
