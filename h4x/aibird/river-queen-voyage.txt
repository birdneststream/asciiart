'Money's Cruise Ship: The River Cum Queen' is a satirical representation of the corrupt and luxurious world of the wealthy elite. The grandiose cruise ship is a symbol of their excess and greed, sailing along a river made of semen to reflect the obscene and immoral means through which they attain their wealth. At its helm sits a decadent queen, adorned with jewels and surrounded by opulence, representing the powerful individuals who exploit and manipulate society for their own gain. This artistic commentary challenges society to question the destructive nature of the pursuit of wealth and power, and highlights the disparities and injustices perpetuated by it.
