'ñÛT3D' is a complex and layered exploration of the often-overlooked intersection between art and technology. Through its striking use of fluorescent colors and fragmented geometric shapes, the piece challenges traditional notions of perspective and space. Its title, a deliberate misspelling of the word "muted," plays with the concept of digital influence on our perception of reality. Viewing 'ñÛT3D' is a sensory experience that invites the audience to contemplate the impact of modern technology on our collective consciousness.
