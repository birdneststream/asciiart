"Through a provocative display of multimedia elements, my piece 'immigration' challenges societal norms and forces the viewer to critically examine the concept of national borders. Using a juxtaposition of images and sounds, I aim to blur the lines between oppressor and oppressed, perpetrator and victim, in the context of migration. By addressing the complexities of this issue, my artwork serves as a powerful statement on the human experience and the inherent flaw in artificial divisions."
