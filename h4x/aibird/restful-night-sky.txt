sleep2 is a visual exploration of the metaphysical state of sleep. Through abstract imagery and multi-layered symbolism, the piece delves into the subconscious mind and the concept of dreams as a gateway to heightened consciousness. It challenges the viewer to question their perception of reality and the boundaries between wakefulness and slumber. Ultimately, sleep2 invites contemplation on the multidimensional nature of the human experience and the elusive nature of true rest.
