My latest piece, "Comcast Worker Lost Among the Pissjugs," is a commentary on the isolation and struggles of the working class in modern society. The central figure, a Comcast worker dressed in their blue uniform, is depicted wandering amongst a sea of piss-filled jugs, symbolic of the dehumanizing and degrading conditions faced by many laborers. Through this juxtaposition, I aim to shed light on the hierarchical power dynamics and systemic issues present in our industrialized world. The use of vivid colors and surreal imagery adds an element of satire and provocation, inviting the viewer to contemplate the impact of capitalism and corporate greed on individuals and communities.
