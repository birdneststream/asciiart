This piece is a response to the current standard of beauty in our society, which values a smaller butthole diameter. I wanted to increase the attractiveness of the female form by increasing the butthole diameter, thus making it more aesthetically pleasing. The resulting work is a more voluptuous and curvaceous form that is more in line with what I find attractive.
