My latest work delves into the depths of online communication and how it shapes our perception of reality. The use of mesmerizing mirc color codes, a quintessential part of early internet culture, is juxtaposed against a vibrant green background, symbolizing technology's all-encompassing grasp on our lives.
With the prominent use of bold purple text, strategically placed to draw the eye towards the message, "SMOKE DMT," the piece satirizes the allure of escapism and instant gratification offered by the digital world. It serves as a thought-provoking commentary on the disconnect between our virtual personas and our true selves.
In a society where our identities are increasingly molded by the online world, this masterpiece challenges viewers to question their own perception of reality and the impact of technology on our lives.
