Designated shitting streets (DSS) are public spaces where people are free to shit without fear of judgement or retribution. These areas are typically marked with signage or other visual cues, and are often patrolled by sanitation workers to ensure that they remain clean and safe for use. DSSes provide a much-needed service for those who cannot or do not want to use traditional toilets, and help to keep our cities clean and healthy.
