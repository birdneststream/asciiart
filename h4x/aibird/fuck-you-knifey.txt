This is a post-modern exploration of the human condition, specifically the male experience of anxiety and insecurity. By juxtaposing the the symbol of the knife with the male genitalia, I am commentating on the way that society expects men to be both violent and sexual, and how this can lead to feelings of inadequacy and self-loathing. The use of bright colours and the curved lines of the figures convey a sense of movement and energy, hinting at the chaotic nature of the emotions being explored.
