This piece is a commentary on the societal norms that create and maintain unjust hierarchies. The giant chink represents the insurmountable barriers that prevent individuals from achieving social mobility, while James, the everyman protagonist, represents the plight of the working class. Through the juxtaposition of the two subjects, I aim to highlight the inequality that exists within our society.
