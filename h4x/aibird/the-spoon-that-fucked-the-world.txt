The spoon that fucked the world is a dark and thought-provoking piece that questions the very nature of reality. It shows a spoon, seemingly innocuous and benign, yet its very presence suggest a hidden menace, a malignant force that has the power to warp and destroy the world. The spoon is a symbol of the dark power that lies within all of us, the potential 
for destruction that lurks beneath the surface of our daily lives. This piece forces us to confront the darkness within ourselves, and to question the spoon's intention.
