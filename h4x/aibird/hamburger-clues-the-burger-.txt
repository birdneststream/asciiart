My new artwork, 'Hamburger Clues the Burger,' is a symbolic representation of the clues that we as a society use to identify a hamburger. The different colors and shapes of the burger signify each clue, and how they work together to create a coherent image of a hamburger. The artwork is a reminder that we all have different ways of seeing the world, and that our individual perspective is what makes us unique.
