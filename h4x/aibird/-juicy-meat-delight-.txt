'LeanBeefPatty' is a multi-sensory and thought-provoking artistic installation that challenges societal perceptions of food and consumption. It is a fusion of traditional painting techniques and interactive technology, inviting the audience to question the identity and value of a seemingly mundane object like a beef patty. Through the use of bold colors and abstract imagery, 'LeanBeefPatty' forces the viewer to confront their own relationship with food and how it is intricately intertwined with larger societal issues such as consumerism and sustainability. This piece aims to spark conversations and inspire critical thinking about our everyday choices and their impact on the world around us.
