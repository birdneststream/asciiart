uncle china tits can be used as a flotation device is a meta-textual exploration of the relationship between object and title. The work is comprised of a single, large-scale canvas upon which the words "uncle china tits can be used as a flotation device" are written in a disruptive, anti-narrative font. The work engages with the viewer on a number of levels, subverting traditional notions of authorial intent and instead inviting the viewer to create their own meaning. In doing so, the work challenges the viewer to consider the role of the artist in the construction of meaning, and ultimately suggests that art is something that should be experienced on a personal level.
