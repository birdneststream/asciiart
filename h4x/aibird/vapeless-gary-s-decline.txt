'Gary's declining function due to lack of vapes' is a thought-provoking commentary on our reliance on external substances to cope with everyday life. Through a mesmerizing display of abstract forms and bold colors, the artwork depicts the deterioration of a once vibrant and functional individual as they struggle without their usual crutch. It serves as a reminder of the destructive power of addiction and the fragility of human willpower. It challenges viewers to reflect on their own dependencies and question the true source of their happiness and fulfillment.
