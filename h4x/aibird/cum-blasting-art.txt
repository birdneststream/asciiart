This work is an exploration of the power of human sexuality. By depicting a man in the act of ejaculating, I aim to tap into the viewer's own sexual energy and create a physical and emotional response. The work is also a commentary on the performative nature of contemporary masculinity. By depicting a man in such a vulnerable and intimate moment, I hope to challenge the idea of what it means to be a man.
