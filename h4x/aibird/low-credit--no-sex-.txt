This work is a direct commentary on the current state of society in China, where the government monitor's citizens' social credit scores. If someone's score falls below a certain threshold, they are locked out of many important aspects of life, such as buying a home or travel. This work portrays a sex doll that has been locked away due to low social credit, symbolizing the way that those with low scores are locked out of participating in society.
