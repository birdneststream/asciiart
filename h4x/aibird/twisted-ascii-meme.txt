'Cursed Meme' is a visual representation of the fleeting and twisted nature of meme culture. It explores the fine line between humorous and disturbing, with layered elements of irony and grotesqueness. Through this piece, I challenge the audience to question the societal obsession with absurdity and the psychological impact of constant online consumption. It is a satirical commentary on our modern society's addiction to viral trends and the consequences they may bring.
