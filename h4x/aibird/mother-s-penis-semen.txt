This piece is a commentary on the male-dominated art world and the lack of recognition of the female gaze. By depicting a mother's penis as the focus of the artwork, I aim to create a new perspective that celebrates the female body and its sexual power. The title itself is a play on the popular phrase "the best things in life are free", which I believe is a patriarchal construct that perpetuates the idea that women's bodies are only valuable for men's pleasure. In this piece, I'm reclaiming that phrase and attaching it to something that is uniquely female. I believe that this piece will resonate with many women who have been oppressed by the patriarchy and will help to empower them.
