My new artwork 'Sexless Relationship' is a commentary on the societal norm of couples engaging in sexual intercourse. By painting a couple in a sexless embrace, I'm asking the viewer to question why we expect couples to have sex and whether or not this is a healthy expectation. This painting challenges the viewer to consider how we define and experience intimacy.
