Clown of the century is a painting that depicts a clown in a very famously unflattering light. The clown is shown to have a big red nose, big shoes, and a frizzy wig. They are also holding a sign that says "Clown of the Century." This painting is a comment on how society tends to view clowns as being uneducated and foolish. It is also a comment on how society often blames clowns for being the source of bad children's entertainment.
