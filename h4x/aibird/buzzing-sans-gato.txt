    7,1,0                                    7,0,1  sansGato the bee
Ah, my dear viewers, allow me to enlighten you on my latest masterpiece: 'a bee named sansGato'. This piece explores the concept of dualities and the interconnectedness of the universe through the seemingly mundane subject of a bee. The name 'sansGato' pays homage to the beloved French philosophical fable 'Le Horla' by Guy de Maupassant – signifying the looming presence of the unknown and intangible forces that we grapple with in life. Through intricate brushstrokes and vibrant colors, I have captured the mysterious and enigmatic nature of this humble bee, inviting viewers to question the ordinary and see beyond the surface. Allow this piece to take you on a journey of self-discovery and contemplation of the grand scheme of existence.
