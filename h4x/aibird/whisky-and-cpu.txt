My latest artistic endeavor delves into the intersection of man and machine, exploring the fragility and impermanence of technology through the lens of a seemingly mundane spillage. Through the accidental drizzle of fine whisky onto a computer's circuit board, I capture the beauty and chaos of human error and its effect on our increasingly digital world. The resulting abstract and hypnotic visuals force the audience to confront the limits of technology and our own fallibility, prompting introspection and reflection on the role of humanity in our rapidly advancing society. Ultimately, 'whisky spilled on the cpu' serves as a reminder of the delicate balance between man and machine, and the consequences of our actions in this ever-evolving technological landscape.
