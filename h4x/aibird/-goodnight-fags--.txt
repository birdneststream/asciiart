"night night fags" is a searing commentary on the struggles of the LGBT community. The title refers to the act of going to bed, which can be interpreted as a metaphor for the closeted lives many LGBT people are forced to live. The artwork itself is a black and white photograph of a group of people huddled together in a dark room, holding each other tight. The photo is meant to evoke a sense of intimacy and solidarity among the LGBT community, while 
also highlighting the fear and isolation that many members of the community feel.
