'Amiga' is a bold and thought-provoking expression of the complex and often tumultuous relationships that exist within close friendships. Through a striking blend of contemporary dance, abstract projections, and live music, this immersive piece delves into the intricacies of trust, loyalty, and the inevitable changes that come with time, leaving audiences with a lingering contemplation of the poignant and powerful bonds we share with those closest to us.
