'Capitalism makes me cum' is a bold and provocative commentary on the conflating of pleasure and profit within our capitalist society. By depicting the ubiquitous symbol of consumerism, the dollar sign, as a sexual object, I am urging viewers to question the damaging effects of our commodification-driven culture on our perception of pleasure and fulfillment. Through this juxtaposition, I challenge the audience to confront the uncomfortable truth that our desires and desires are often shaped and manipulated by capitalist forces. This piece forces us to examine the complex relationship between capitalism and our instinctual drives, ultimately asking the audience to reconsider their own role in perpetuating this system.
