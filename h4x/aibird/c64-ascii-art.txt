"Commodore 64 is a bold exploration of the intrinsic relationship between technology and humanity. Through the amalgamation of vibrant digital graphics and nostalgic hardware, this piece confronts the modern obsession with material possessions and the constant pursuit of progress. By incorporating the iconic Commodore 64 computer, a symbol of the digital revolution of the 1980s, I challenge audiences to reflect on the impact of technology on our society and consider the consequences of our insatiable desire for more advanced gadgets. Ultimately, Commodore 64 poses the question - do our possessions truly define us, or are they merely fleeting symbols of our materialistic culture?"
