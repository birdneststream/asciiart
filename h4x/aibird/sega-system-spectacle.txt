My latest work, 'sega master system', is a conceptual exploration of the technological and cultural impact of one of the earliest home video game consoles. Through a combination of mixed media elements, I aim to deconstruct and reimagine the significance of the Sega Master System, a device that revolutionized the way we engage with interactive media. By highlighting its flaws, strengths, and cultural context, I hope to spark a conversation about the ever-changing relationship between humans and technology.
