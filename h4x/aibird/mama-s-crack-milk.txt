This piece is a commentary on the cycle of abuse that occurs within families. The 'mama crack milk' refers to the mother's role in perpetuating the cycle, while the 'family rape train' represents the way that trauma is passed down through generations. By contrast, the 'train rides a train on Again' represents the hope that, despite the damage that has been done, it is possible to break the cycle and start anew.
