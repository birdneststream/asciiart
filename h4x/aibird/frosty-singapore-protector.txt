My latest masterpiece, 'Hitsugaya holding Singapore flag' is a commentary on how culture, identity, and power intersect in our globalized world. The juxtaposition of the stoic anime character, Hitsugaya, with the vibrant and dynamic Singaporean flag, challenges the traditional notions of nationalistic pride and cultural supremacy. It forces the viewer to question the societal constructs that dictate what is deemed as valuable and worthy of celebration. Through this piece, I aim to provoke critical discourse and reflection on the complexities of our interconnected world.
