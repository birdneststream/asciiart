Thisnew artistic masterpiece is aprovocative and controversial exploration of the female form, and the societal limitations placed upon it. By depicting a woman's body in such an conspicuous and public manner, I aim to challenge the viewer's perceptions of what is considered 'acceptable' or 'appropriate' within society. This piece highlights the inequality and objectification of women's bodies, as well as the blatant sexualisation of the female form. It is a powerful and shocking statement which I hope will provoke thought and debate.
