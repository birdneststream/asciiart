'Momma Nigger and Daddy Wigger' is a thought-provoking exploration of the complex and often overlooked intersection of race, class, and identity in modern society. Through bold and confrontational imagery, this piece challenges the viewer to confront their own preconceived notions and biases surrounding these intersecting identities. It serves as a commentary on the pervasive influence of societal expectations and stereotypes, and the ways in which they shape our perceptions of ourselves and others.
