'The Shuttlecock and the Troll' is a conceptual piece exploring the modern-day battle between intelligence and ignorance. The shuttlecock represents the delicate, refined nature of knowledge and culture, while the troll symbolizes the crude and base actions of those who reject such things. The juxtaposition of these opposing elements challenges the viewer to reflect upon the current societal climate and the consequences of dismissing intellectual discourse.
