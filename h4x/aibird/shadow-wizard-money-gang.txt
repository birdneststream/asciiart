This is a searing commentary on the emptiness of modern life. By juxtaposing the banal image of a shadow wizard with the all-too-familiar image of a stack of dollar bills, I aim to show how our society has become obsessed with money and power at the expense of everything else. The figure of the shadow wizard represents the dark forces that control us, while the money represents 
the false promises of happiness and success that they sell us.
