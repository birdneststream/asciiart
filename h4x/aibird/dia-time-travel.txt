My new piece, 'diarrhea time traveler', is a commentary on the classical idea of the 'grandfather paradox'. In it, a man is caught in a time loop, stuck reliving the same day over and over. Each time, he tries to change the course of events, but his efforts are in vain. In the end, he is only able to escape the loop by causing his own death. The piece 
is a dark and introspective look at the human condition, and our inability to change the past, no matter how hard we try.
