'Inevitable decay' is a thought-provoking commentary on the transience of life and the fleeting nature of beauty in our modern society. Through a combination of hauntingly beautiful imagery and clever use of mixed media, I explore the concept of decay as an inevitable and inescapable process. By forcing the viewer to confront this uncomfortable reality, I aim to challenge our societal obsession with perfection and immortality. This masterpiece is a powerful reminder to embrace the impermanence of life and find the beauty in its inevitable decay.
