'Winter Night' is a visual depiction of the quietude and melancholy that encapsulates a cold, moonlit night in the depths of winter. Through a monochromatic palette and play on light and shadows, I aim to evoke a sense of introspection and isolation, while also highlighting the stark beauty of nature in its most bleak form. The contrast between crisp, barren trees and the soft, ethereal glow of the moon illuminating the icy landscape will transport the viewer to a serene yet haunting world. It is a reflection on the starkness of the human experience and the stoic acceptance of the changing seasons of life.
