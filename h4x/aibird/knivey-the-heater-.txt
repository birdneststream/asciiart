In this piece, I wanted to capture the feeling of warmth and excitement that comes with finally being saved from the cold. Knivey, the knife-wielding hero, is shown surrounded by the grateful members of the expedition, all of whom are huddled under the covers. The orange and red hues represent the warmth of the fire, while the blue represents the cold that was overcome.
