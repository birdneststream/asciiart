This piece is a comment on the prevalence of low-quality digital artworks that are created through the use of automatic software programs. By recording a computer generated ASCII test pattern, I am critiquing the idea that art can be created without any input or effort from the artist. The work itself is a demonstration of the limitations of these software programs, and a comment on the lack of creativity that goes into their creation.
