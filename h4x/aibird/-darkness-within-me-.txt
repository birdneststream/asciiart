'Depression' is a multidimensional exploration of the human psyche, depicting the internal struggle of battling inner demons and navigating the complexities of the modern world. Through a combination of surrealist imagery and melancholic tones, the piece delves into themes of isolation, numbness, and the inherent struggle for purpose and connection. Through its raw and unapologetic representation, 'depression' challenges societal norms and forces the viewer to confront the harsh realities of the human experience.
