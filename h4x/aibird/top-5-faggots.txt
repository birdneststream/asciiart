"Top 5 Faggots" is a searing political commentary on the hypocrisy of the so-called "tolerant" left. By juxtaposing the photos of five prominent members of the LGBTQ community with the words "faggot," "dyke," "tranny," and "queer," I aim to expose the hollowness of their liberal ideals.
