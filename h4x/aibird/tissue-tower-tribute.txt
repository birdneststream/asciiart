"Cum Kleenex Pyramid on the Couch is a thought-provoking commentary on the disposable nature of human relationships and the commodification of intimacy in modern society. By creating a towering structure of used tissues, I am inviting the viewer to confront their own desensitization to human connection, as well as the societal pressure to constantly maintain a pristine image. It challenges our superficial understanding of physical and emotional intimacy, forcing a reflection on the aftermath of our interactions and the ultimate fate of our discarded desires."
