'Ty for the +v' is a commentary on the social media culture and the superficial nature of online interaction. The simplistic yet cryptic title serves as a nod to the shorthand language commonly used on social platforms. Through a mixed media installation, I have curated a visually striking representation of the incessant need for validation and the endless cycle of seeking approval through virtual validation. The juxtaposition of emojis, hashtags, and likes with traditional art elements challenges the viewers to question the impact of technology on human connection and self-worth. Overall, 'ty for the +v' invites a reflection on our dependence on digital affirmation and its consequences on our perception of reality.
