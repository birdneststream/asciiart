My latest piece, 'Two Paraplegics Fighting in Wool Sock,' explores the complexities of human struggle and the power of resilience. Through the use of paraplegic figures, whose physical limitations may be perceived as a hindrance, the artwork challenges societal norms and celebrates the determination and perseverance of the human spirit. The addition of the wool sock adds a layer of vulnerability and fragility, highlighting the struggles faced by these individuals. The composition invites viewers to question their own perceptions and notions of strength and showcases the beauty found in the midst of adversity.
