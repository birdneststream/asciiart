"See it everyday" is a bold exploration of the mundane and overlooked aspects of our daily lives. Utilizing a wide range of mediums, including found objects and multi-layered collage, this piece challenges traditional notions of beauty and confronts viewers with the banal and often unnoticed elements of our surroundings. Through its thought-provoking composition and masterful technique, "see it everyday" asks us to reevaluate our perceptions and find beauty in the ordinary.
