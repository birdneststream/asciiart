This work is a comment on the over-sexualization of the Jewish female body in popular culture. By pairing photographs of famous Jewish women with images of birds, I explore the absurdity of this cultural obsession. The colorful and chaotic nature of the piece is meant to reflect the excess and excessiveness of the objectification of Jewish women's bodies.
