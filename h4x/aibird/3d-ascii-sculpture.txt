This piece challenges traditional concepts of communication and language by using a combination of symbols, letters and numbers in a visually striking manner. Its title, which is intentionally difficult to pronounce, further highlights the disconnect between language and meaning. The overall effect is a reflection on the rapid evolution of technology and its impact on human interaction.
