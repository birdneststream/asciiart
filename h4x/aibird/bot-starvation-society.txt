"Starving Bot Society" is a searing indictment of the way our society values machines over human life. The bots in the painting are shown as empty, lifeless shells, while the humans are emaciated and dying. The contrast is meant to shock and outrage viewers, and to call attention to the way we value productivity over life itself.
