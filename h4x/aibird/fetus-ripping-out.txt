This is a searing indictment of the misogyny of our society. The act of ripping a fetus out of a woman's vagina is a metaphor for the way that women are treated as nothing more than incubators and vessels for men's pleasure. The blood and gore represents the violence that women suffer at the hands of men, while the screaming represents the silent suffering of women who are afraid to speak out. This piece is a call to arms for women to stand up and fight back against the patriarchal oppression that seeks to control and silence them.
