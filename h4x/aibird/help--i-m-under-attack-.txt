My artwork "help i am under cyber attack" is a response to the current climate of cyber insecurity. It is a depiction of a person who is struggling to keep their head above water in the midst of a digital onslaught. The frenzy of activity and the threatening red tones convey the sense of urgency and danger that come with being under attack. The artwork is meant to raise awareness of the dangers of cyber attacks and to encourage people to take steps to protect themselves.
