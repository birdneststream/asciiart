'East_atlanta_santa has the most handsome internal organs' is a visually arresting exploration of societal norms and superficiality. Through stark contrasts of beauty and the grotesque, the piece challenges the viewer to question the value placed on external appearance versus internal complexities. With meticulous attention to detail, the artwork presents a hauntingly beautiful examination of the human form that will leave a lasting impact on all who experience it.
