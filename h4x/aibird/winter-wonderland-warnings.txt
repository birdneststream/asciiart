My new artistic masterpiece is a comment on the irony of the phrase "White Christmas" as it evokes images of purity and innocence, while excluding and marginalizing certain groups of people with derogatory terms. By purposely omitting these words from the title, I am highlighting the impact of language and the importance of inclusion and respect in our society. Through this piece, I hope to challenge the societal norms and prejudices embedded in our language and bring attention to the power of words in shaping our perceptions of race and identity.
