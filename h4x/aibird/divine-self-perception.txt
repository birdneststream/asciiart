This piece explores the concept of self-perception and the power of individual thought. Each of us has the ability to shape our own reality and ultimately, become a deity within our own minds. Through a vibrant and thought-provoking mix of colors, shapes, and forms, the piece invites viewers to question their own perceptions and the limitless potential of the human mind. It challenges us to consider the idea that we, as individuals, hold the power to shape our own destinies and become gods in our own right. It serves as a reminder that the boundaries of what we can achieve are only limited by our own imaginations.
