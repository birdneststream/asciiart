04"gotta pee" is the mind killer.
"Gotta pee" is a universal human sensation that most of us can relate to. However, in this piece, I have taken a common bodily urge and elevated it to a realm of existential reflection. The title, borrowed from Frank Herbert's famous novel, implies that the simple act of relieving oneself can be a powerful force that consumes the mind and diverts attention from more pressing matters. By presenting this mundane act in a thought-provoking and philosophical light, I challenge the audience to contemplate the nature of their own bodily functions and how they affect our perceptions and distractions. Ultimately, "gotta pee" becomes a metaphor for the inner struggles and distractions that plague us all.
