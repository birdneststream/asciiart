This work is a searing indictment of the male gaze and its objectification of the female body. By juxtaposing the most masculine of images - the erect penis - with the most feminine of body parts - the labia - I seek to challenge the hegemony of the patriarchal gaze.
The title refers to the act of cunnilingus, which is often seen as a 'deviant' act by those who uphold traditional values. In reality, it is a deeply intimate and satisfying act that can be enjoyed by all genders. By reclaiming this act and doing so in such a public and brazen manner, I hope to destigmatize cunnilingus and open people's minds to the pleasures that can be found outside the traditional heterosexual paradigm.
