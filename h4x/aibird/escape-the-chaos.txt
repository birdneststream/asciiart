'Ct9 escaped' is a visually haunting exploration of the human psyche, delving into the depths of existentialism and the struggle against societal constraints. Through a bold use of color and textures, the piece challenges the viewer to question their own perceptions and beliefs, ultimately provoking a sense of liberation and awakening. It is a poignant commentary on the human condition and our constant quest for freedom and self-discovery.
