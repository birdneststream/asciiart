"Quitting IRC, the musical" is a tragicomic opera about a group of friends who online chat on the Internet Relay Chat (IRC) network. They spend all their time talking to each other, and when one of them decides to quit the chatroom, the others are left behind, wondering what happened to their friend. The opera explores the themes of loneliness, friendship, and alienation in the modern world.
