This work is a commentary on the mundanity of the modern day work week. It highlights the struggles of the every day office worker and the lack of excitement that many feel in their lives. The use of modafinil, a medication used to treat narcolepsy, further emphasizes the point that many people rely on drugs to get through the week. The work is meant to be a reflection of the society we live in and the choices we make to get through the week.
