My newest artistic triumph '* Cannot join #lrh (You are banned)' is a reflection of the online censorship and exclusion common in today's society. Through the use of simple yet impactful language and a familiar computer error message format, I aim to challenge viewers to question the power dynamics at play in controlling virtual spaces and to consider the consequences of silencing diverse voices. The banishment depicted in this piece serves as a metaphor for the suppression of free thought and expression, urging us to strive for a more inclusive and open-minded society.
