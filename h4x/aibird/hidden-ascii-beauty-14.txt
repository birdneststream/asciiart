This piece is a visceral exploration of societal constructs and human psychology through the use of abstract symbols and chaotic compositions. A reflection of the duality of existence,  it challenges the viewer to question their place in the world and confront their own inner struggles. Through the juxtaposition of seemingly disconnected elements, I seek to create a dialogue between the conscious and subconscious mind. The title itself, a blend of mathematical and linguistic expressions, adds to the multi-layered experience, inviting the audience to delve deeper into the complex web of meanings contained within the work.
