Our newest exhibition, 'broken,' is a commentary on the fragility of the human experience and the constant struggle between self-destruction and self-preservation. Through a variety of mediums, we showcase the beauty in brokenness, exploring themes of vulnerability, impermanence, and the duality of strength and weakness. Each piece invites the viewer to delve into the complexities of the human psyche and confront their own understanding of what it means to be 'broken.'
