"Bold Box Bliss is a multifaceted exploration of the human desire for ordered chaos. Using a vibrant color palette and bold geometric shapes, this piece challenges the viewer to question the boundaries between structure and chaos. By interpolating layers of texture and highlighting contrasting hues, I aimed to create a visual representation of the dichotomy between our need for control and the liberating bliss of letting go."
