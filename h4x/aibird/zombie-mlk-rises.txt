This piece is a commentary on race relations in America. It is an exploration of what might happen if zombie MLK were to return from the grave and start a race war. The title is a play on words, substituting the word " whites" for " May Day." The piece is acrylic on canvas, and is meant to be viewed from left to right. It is a warning to the viewer 
that the situation is not as benign as it seems, and that the zombie apocalypse is a very real possibility.
