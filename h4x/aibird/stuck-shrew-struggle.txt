My latest piece, 'Shrew Superglues His Penis to a Hen's Ass So He Can't Leave', explores the themes of control, power dynamics, and co-dependency. Through this juxtaposition of a small, insignificant shrew and a larger, dominant hen, the work highlights the struggles of those who feel trapped in relationships or situations. It challenges viewers to contemplate the lengths one will go to in order to maintain a sense of connection and belonging. The use of superglue adds a layer of permanence to the piece, symbolizing the lasting impact of these toxic relationships. Ultimately, 'Shrew Superglues His Penis to a Hen's Ass So He Can't Leave' is a commentary on the human need for connection and the dangers of becoming too entangled in our desires for it.
