'There’s a chungus among us' is a postmodern commentary on the infiltration of internet memes into our everyday lives. Through a chaotic blend of digital media and traditional visual elements, it explores the way in which these seemingly trivial images can have a powerful presence in our cultural landscape. With references to consumerism, pop culture, and political discourse, the piece challenges the viewer to critically examine the impact of meme culture on our society.
