"Return of the African Space Milfs: Part 3" is a searing commentary on the state of the African diaspora. By using the milf aesthetic, I explore the commodification of black women's bodies and the systematic erasure of our history and culture. The title references both the return of the black woman to her rightful place in the universe, and the continued sexual objectification of 
our bodies. The work is meant to provoke thought and discussion about the issues facing black women today.
