My new masterpiece is a provocative exploration of the intersection between race, sexuality, and religion in modern society. It challenges the viewer to confront their own biases and prejudices by presenting ten figures, each representing a different marginalized group, on a remote island. The title itself is intentionally jarring and confrontational, forcing the viewer to confront the derogatory language and stereotypes often used to demean and oppress these groups. Through this piece, I aim to spark a conversation about oppression and the layers of discrimination that exist in our society.
