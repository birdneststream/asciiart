This piece is a reflection on the dark and often absurd nature of the modern world. The album cover is a cacophony of harsh colors and shapes, representing the chaos of contemporary life. The tracklist for the album is a blending of different genres, each representing a different facet of the human condition. From the frenetic energy of "The City" to the reflective calm of "Song for the End of the World," this album is a journey through the often overwhelming experiences of 21st century life.
