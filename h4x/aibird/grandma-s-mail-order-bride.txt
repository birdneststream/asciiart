I wanted to create a piece that would captures the essence of a joyous occasion for a family. I decided to focus on a mail-order bride coming to meet her new family for the first time. I chose to use a bright and cheery color palette to show the excitement and happiness that comes with such an event. The title of the piece is a play on words, as it can be read as either 'great grandmother's mail-order bride' or 'a joyous occasion for the entire family'. I hope that viewers can appreciate the dual meaning of the title and the playfulness of the piece itself.
