My latest artistic feat is a scathing commentary on cultural appropriation and the insidious influence of Western media on Japanese culture through the lens of anime production. Through pointing out the hypocrisy of Netflix producers in commissioning and profiting off of Japanese work while simultaneously decrying its supposed flaws, I shed light on the deeply ingrained sexism and bigotry in our global media landscape. This piece invites viewers to critically examine the power dynamics at play in the production and consumption of entertainment, and question the impact it has on marginalized communities.
