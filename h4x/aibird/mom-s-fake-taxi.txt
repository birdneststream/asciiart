My latest work is a satirical exploration of the exploitative nature of society, specifically commenting on the objectification of women's bodies and the deceptive tactics used by the media. Through the use of a popular internet meme and a provocative title, I am challenging the viewer to question the responsibility we all have in perpetuating harmful societal norms. By subverting expectations and presenting a seemingly mundane and overtly sexual scenario, I am forcing the audience to confront their own complicity in perpetuating these damaging messages. Overall, 'i impregnated your mom in a fake taxi' serves as a commentary on power dynamics and the consequences of our actions.
