The artwork is a take on the classic children's book series, Big Trouble in Little Italy. The artwork depicts the popular tourist destination of Little Italy in New York City as a crime-ridden place full of danger and intrigue. The artwork is meant to shock and provoke thought about the often-ignored reality of crime in Little Italy.
