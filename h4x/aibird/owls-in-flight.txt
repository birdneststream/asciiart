This piece is a commentary on the absurdities of modern society. By depicting a scene of owls flying out of a bedroom window, I'm pointing to the ways in which we are constantly bombarded with information and stimuli. The owls represent the never-ending stream of thoughts and ideas that keep us up at night, preventing us from getting a good night's sleep. The title refers to the fact that there are an estimated one billion owls in the world, which is a lot of owls.
