My new artistic masterpiece, "An Enlarged Prostate is Just More to Please," challenges society's notion of masculinity and pleasure by highlighting the often-dismissed experience of aging men – an enlarged prostate. By enlarging this typically hidden and stigmatized organ, I aim to bring attention to the beauty and complexity of the male body and its functions. Through this piece, I hope to start a conversation about ableism and ageism within our society, ultimately advocating for the celebration of all bodies and experiences.
