IRC, or Internet Relay Chat, is a protocol for communication over the internet. It allows users to communicate with each other in real time without needing to use a third-party service. 
However, recently, snitches have been ruining IRC for everyone. These snitches are people who report others for using offensive or inappropriate language, or for breaking the rules of the chatroom. This has resulted in many users being banned from IRC, and has made the chatrooms less enjoyable for everyone. 
I created this artwork to express my frustration with the snitches who are ruining IRC. The image shows a group of people being dragged away by a group of snitches, while the rest of the users watch in horror. This reflects the feeling of helplessness that many users experience when they see others being banned for no reason. 
I hope that this artwork willraise awareness of the problem of snitches on IRC, and help to make the chatrooms more enjoyable for everyone.
