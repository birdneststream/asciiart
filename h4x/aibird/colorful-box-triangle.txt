My new piece delves into the concept of symbolism and the power of colors to convey meaning. The vibrant red and yellow box serves as a representation of consumerism and the saturation of capitalism in our society. The addition of the pink triangle adds a layer of commentary on LGBTQ+ rights and the ongoing fight for equality. By combining these elements, I hope to provoke thought and discussion on the complex issues that intertwine in our modern world.
