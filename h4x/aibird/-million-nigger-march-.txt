This piece is a powerful and provocative statement about the state of race relations in America today. The title, "Million Nigger March", refers to the large number of African Americans who are marching for equality and justice. The image itself is a striking visualization of the unity and strength of the black community. The marching figures are outlined in white, which represents the power and purity of their cause. The use of the word "nigger" in the title is intentional and shocking, meant to jars viewers into paying attention to the very real problem of racism in our country.
