Goodnight chat pals!
'Goodnight Chat Pals' is a thought-provoking and dynamic commentary on the intimate connections and fleeting nature of online friendships. Through mixed media techniques, including text, digital imagery, and traditional painting, it portrays the bittersweet moments of bidding farewell to dear chat companions before signing off for the night. The artwork challenges our perception of modern communication and invites viewers to reflect on the meaningful relationships forged in the virtual world.
