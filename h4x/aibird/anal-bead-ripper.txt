This piece examines the concept of masculinity and dominance through the use of a seemingly mundane act - starting a lawnmower - juxtaposed with a taboo sexual act - the ripping out of anal beads. By placing these two elements together, the artist challenges societal norms and expectations of gender roles, while also delving into the raw and often overlooked side of human sexuality. The repetitive motion of the act also speaks to larger societal issues such as power dynamics and control.
