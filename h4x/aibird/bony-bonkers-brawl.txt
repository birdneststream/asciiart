Bone Headed Boondoggle is a satirical commentary on the ridiculousness of political and social norms. Through a combination of multimedia elements, the piece explores the concept of blindly following authority, represented by a looming skeleton with a politician's head and a backdrop of chaotic, muddled images. It is a scathing critique of the absurdity and dangers of blindly following leaders who have no regard for the wellbeing of their constituents. With dark humor and a touch of absurdity, it challenges viewers to question their own complacency and the consequences of being a "boneheaded" follower in a wild, misguided "boondoggle" of society.
