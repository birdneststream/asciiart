Sarah Connor is a provocative and thought-provoking exploration of the complexities of defining one's own identity. Through a visually striking combination of mixed media, including paint, found materials, and digital collage, Connor challenges societal norms and biases surrounding gender, power, and femininity. The fragmented and layered composition serves as a metaphor for the fragmented nature of one's self, constantly being shaped by external forces. Through this powerful piece, the audience is forced to confront their own perceptions and limitations, ultimately questioning the very nature of what it means to be human.
