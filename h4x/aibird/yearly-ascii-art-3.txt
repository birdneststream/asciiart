My new piece is a representation of the passage of time and how it affects us all. The title, "ÅrS o," is a play on words, combining the word for "years" in Swedish with the symbol for the empty space between moments. The visual elements of the piece, with its sparse and disjointed composition, convey a sense of fragmentation and the fleeting nature of time. The use of different symbols, including a series of stacked blocks and a unique font for the numbers, adds to the overall complexity of the work. Through this piece, I hope to invite viewers to contemplate their own relationship with time and how it shapes their lives.
