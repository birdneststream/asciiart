"Polka Dot Zebra is a visual exploration of the dualities within our society, displayed through the juxtaposition of traditional African wildlife and a modern, playful element of polka dots. The zebra, a symbol of freedom and individuality, is adorned with the seemingly childish polka dots, representing our societal pressures to conform and fit into a rigid mold. This piece challenges the viewer to question the balance between self-expression and societal norms in a visually stunning and thought-provoking manner."
