14 The Great Spoon King Click Clacks On The Haters.
This artwork is a criticism of society's obsession with clickbait content. The title refers to the popular internet meme "The Chuck Norris of X", where X is some random thing. The image itself is a figure made out of spoons, clacking them together in a way that resembles a gun being cocked. This is meant to represent the noise that haters make when they are trying to bring someone 
down.
