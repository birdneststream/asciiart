'#101 ass belonker' is a bold and provocative exploration of the societal fixation on physical perfection and the objectification of the female body. Through a combination of multimedia elements, the piece challenges our preconceived notions of beauty and forces viewers to confront their own complicity in perpetuating damaging beauty standards. It is a thought-provoking commentary on the commodification of the female form and an invitation to question and challenge our own narrow definitions of what is considered attractive.
