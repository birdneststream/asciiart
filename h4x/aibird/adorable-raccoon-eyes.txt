'again has the cutest little raccoon eyes' is a visual exploration of the endearing qualities of the natural world and the innocence and purity that can be found in unexpected places. Through the use of intricate brushstrokes and subtle symbolism, I aim to challenge societal norms and redefine beauty while also invoking a nostalgic sense of wonder within the viewer. This piece celebrates the unique and charming qualities of the raccoon species, reminding us to appreciate and cherish all forms of life.
