The imagery is meant to shock and disgust viewers, while also providing a pointed commentary on the ways in which the trucking industry mistreats its employees and customers. The artist behind "Cuck Truckers" is making a bold statement about the need for reform within the trucking industry, and it is sure to provoke strong reactions from both supporters and detractors.
