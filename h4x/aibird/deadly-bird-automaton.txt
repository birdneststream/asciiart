'Automatic bird killer' is a powerful commentary on the destructive and senseless nature of technological advancement. It depicts a machine that mindlessly snuffs out the existence of innocent birds, representing the inevitable harm and disregard for nature that comes with our unquenchable pursuit of progress. Through its jarring imagery and provocative title, the piece challenges viewers to confront the consequences of our relentless pursuit of efficiency and advancement at any cost.
