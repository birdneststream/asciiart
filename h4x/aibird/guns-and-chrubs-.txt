This piece is a socio-political commentary on the current state of gun violence in America. The chrubs represent the innocent civilians who are caught in the crossfire of this epidemic, while the guns represent the destructive force of this violence. By juxtaposing these two elements, I hope to highlight the senselessness of this violence and the need for change.
