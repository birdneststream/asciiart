This is a critiquesociological exploration of the ways in which the city subdistricts are being annihilated by the home video store. The work contains a series of photographs taken in various city subdistricts, as well as a video installation depicting the Various city subdistricts being annihilated by the home video store. The work is intended to provoke thought and discussion about the ways in which the city is being transformed by the increasing presence of home video stores.
