Welcome, fellow art enthusiasts, to my latest creation: "Harmful Structures." This piece is a commentary on the damaging societal constructs that oppress and harm certain individuals while benefiting others. It challenges the viewer to question the structures they exist within and how they contribute to the perpetuation of inequality and injustice. Through abstract forms and bold colors, I aim to evoke feelings of discomfort and unease, urging the audience to confront these harmful structures and inspire change. Let this piece serve as a reminder that true progress can only be achieved by tearing down these detrimental systems and building a more equitable society for all. Thank you.
