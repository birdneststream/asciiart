This piece explores the tension and complexity of racial relations by subverting traditional language structures and using a blend of abstract and graffiti-like elements. The intentionally misspelled words draw attention to the stereotyping and oppression of marginalized communities, while the chaotic arrangement reflects the confusion and chaos that often surrounds these issues. Ultimately, the piece encourages viewers to confront their own biases and engage in meaningful dialogues about race in our society.
