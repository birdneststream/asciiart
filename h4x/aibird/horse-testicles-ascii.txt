This new work is a exploration of the intersection of the natural world and the man-made world. By juxtaposing horse testicles with a backdrop of the suburban sprawl, I'm asking viewers to consider the ways in which we have domesticated and objectified nature. The work is also a commentary on the way in which the male gaze often dictate how we see and interpret the world around us.
