This piece is a commentary on the growing trend of people using social media to objectify and exploit women's bodies. The title refers to the popular Instagram account of a woman who frequently posts photos and videos of herself in sexualized poses and engaged in sexual activity. The figure in the painting is based on one of the photos from this account. By painting her in a more 
traditional, classical style, I am highlighting the contrast between the timeless beauty of the human form and the ephemeral, exploitative nature of social media.
