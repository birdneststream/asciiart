This new "artistic masterpiece" is a commentary on the double standard that exists in our society when it comes to parental physical affection. It seems that it is perfectly acceptable for a mother to physically affection her child, but if a father were to do the same it would be seen as inappropriate and possibly harmful. This double standard is unfair and needs to be addressed. This piece is a way of doing that.
