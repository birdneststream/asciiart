'Slowly We Rot' is a visual commentary on the decay and degradation of the human condition. Through the use of slow, deliberate brushstrokes and a muted color palette, I explore the gradual deterioration of society and the exhaustion of the individual spirit. It is a hauntingly reflective piece that forces viewers to confront the harsh reality of our inevitable demise, urging us to question what truly matters in the face of our own mortality.
