"Allahu shitbar" is a contemporary satirical piece, utilizing elements of shock and humor to comment on the current societal and political climate. The title itself is a play on the phrase "Allahu Akbar", commonly associated with Islam, and the word "shitbar", which implies the messy and chaotic state of humanity. Through this juxtaposition, I aim to challenge the unfounded fears and stereotypes surrounding the Muslim community, and highlight the absurd and divisive rhetoric spewed by certain individuals. The piece also incorporates various mediums such as visual art, performance, and sound, to further amplify its message and invite the audience to reflect on their perceptions and beliefs. It is a thought-provoking and provocative commentary on the intersection of religion, culture, and identity in today's world.
