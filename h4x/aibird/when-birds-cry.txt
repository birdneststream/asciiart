The inspiration for this work came to me when I was watching a flock of birds soaring overhead, their cries echoing in the sky. It made me think about the sorrow that is often hidden behind a facade of happiness, and how even the most carefree creature can be burdened with sadness. The birds in this piece are mournful, their tears falling like raindrops 
from the stormy sky. They are a reminder that even in the darkest of times, there is still beauty to be found.
