My new artistic masterpiece 'jewish bird tribe' is based on the ancient story of the Jew and the Bird. In this story, a group of birds live near a Jewish community and are constantly being pestered by the Jews. One day, the birds decide to fly away and live in peace. However, one of the birds, the smallest and weakest of the group, is left behind. The bird is taken in by the Jews and eventually comes to love them. The moral of the story is that even the weakest and most vulnerable among us can find a place of acceptance and love.
