'Birdnest' captures the essence of both nature and human influence. Through a paradoxical blend of fragile bird nests and sturdy man-made wires, this installation comments on the delicate balance between civilization and the natural world. The juxtaposition of intricate, organic forms with harsh industrial elements invites viewers to contemplate our impact on the environment and our coexistence with other creatures. It serves as a reminder that our actions have consequences and the need for harmony with nature.
