"Manifesting social norms and expectations, my latest work deconstructs the traditional dynamics of elderly intimacy through a playful yet provocative lens. By juxtaposing the innocence of a sleeping grandpa with the raw and unapologetic act of grandma's squirting, I challenge societal constructs surrounding age and sexuality. Through the use of unexpected textures and elements, such as a cheesy pillow, I encourage viewers to question their own biases and embrace the sensual nature of aging. This piece blurs the lines between humor and discomfort, ultimately sparking dialogue and reflection on our ingrained perceptions of intimacy."
