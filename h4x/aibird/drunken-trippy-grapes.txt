My new artistic masterpiece is a hyperrealistic interpretation of the modern technological world and its effects on our consciousness. Through a chaotic blend of symbols such as DMT, LSD, ANSI, PP4L, Reddit, AOL, ICQ, Yahoo pool, grapes, window air conditioner, high res, low quality, and 72 virgins, I invite the viewer to question the dizzying overload of information and perspectives that bombard us on a daily basis. The inclusion of psychedelic substances and references to internet culture serves as a commentary on the search for altered states of mind and escapism in the digital age. The juxtaposition of high resolution and low quality elements mirrors the constant fluctuation between the virtual and the real world. Ultimately, this piece challenges the audience to confront the disorienting and at times destructive consequences of our obsession with technology.
