'Insomnia' is a visual commentary on the battle between productivity and rest in our fast-paced society. The piece showcases a chaotic cityscape, filled with fluorescent lights and haphazardly stacked buildings, representing the constant noise and distractions of our modern lives. Amidst this chaos, a lone figure tosses and turns, unable to find solace in sleep. This represents the struggle to find peace and balance amidst the constant pressure to be productive and successful. With bold colors and abstract shapes, 'Insomnia' challenges viewers to confront the exhausting effects of our society's obsession with productivity.
