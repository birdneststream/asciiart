This piece is a commentary on society's neglect of the mentally ill. By juxtaposing the severed paw of a possum with the notion of making wishes, I aim to highlight the importance of caring for those who are often unseen and forgotten. The title itself is a play on words, hinting at the misplaced focus on the superficial and materialistic things in life rather than on the things that truly matter.
