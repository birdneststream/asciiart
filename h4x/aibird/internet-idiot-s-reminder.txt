"Never Ever Go Full Retard on the Internet" is a satirical exploration of the dangers of internet culture and the decline of critical thinking online. Through a fusion of multimedia elements such as memes, viral videos, and screenshots, the piece exposes the absurdity of extreme behavior and extreme views in the digital realm. It serves as a cautionary reminder to stay vigilant and question the information presented to us, as well as to not lose our sense of reason and humanity in the sea of online chaos.
