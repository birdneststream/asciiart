"Owlcult is a provocative exploration of the human fascination with power, symbolism, and cult mentality. With striking imagery and thought-provoking symbolism, this piece challenges societal norms and shines a light on humanity's tendency to blindly follow and worship figures of authority. Through the use of intricate owl motifs and a bold color palette, Owlcult urges viewers to question their own beliefs and the dangers of unquestioned devotion."
