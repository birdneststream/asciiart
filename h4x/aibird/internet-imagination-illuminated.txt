"All memes will become dreams" is a thought-provoking work that explores the ephemeral nature of internet culture and its impact on our collective consciousness. Through a combination of colorful imagery and cryptic symbolism, I aim to challenge the viewer to reflect on the transience of viral content and question the value we place on it. This piece serves as a commentary on the constantly evolving nature of modern communication and encourages us to contemplate the blurred lines between reality and the digital realm."
