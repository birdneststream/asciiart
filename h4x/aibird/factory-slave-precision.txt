My latest piece, entitled "Chinese Factory Slave," depicts the profound impact of globalization on labor and human rights. It features a lone worker, tirelessly arranging each individual strand for a plywood board in a factory setting. This powerful image speaks to the exploitation and dehumanization inherent in our global economy, prompting viewers to confront their own complicity in a system that perpetuates such injustice. Through a skilled use of composition, color, and texture, I aim to evoke a sense of empathy and provoke a critical dialogue about the true cost of our material possessions.
