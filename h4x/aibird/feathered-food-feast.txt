Bird Beak Buffet is a modern commentary on consumerism and excess, focusing on the concept of feeding frenzy. The installation features a large buffet table covered in intricate bird beak sculptures made from discarded human waste, representing the insatiable appetite of society for material goods. The beaks are arranged in a chaotic manner, symbolizing the chaos and destruction caused by overconsumption. Viewers are invited to partake in the buffet, physically engaging them in the message of the piece. Through this intimate interaction, the work asks viewers to question their own behaviors and the impact on the world around them.
