System Demons is a work of art that explores the darker side of humanity. It is a damning critique of the way we have organized our societies, and the Weaponization of everything within them. 
The work consists of a series of images that depict the various facets of our society that have been corrupted by the quest for power and profit. From the military-industrial complex to the financial sector, no institution is safe from the demons that have taken hold of our world. 
The work is meant to shock and disturb, but also to spark a necessary conversation about the direction our world is heading in. We must confront these demons if we are to have any hope of saving ourselves from self-destruction.
