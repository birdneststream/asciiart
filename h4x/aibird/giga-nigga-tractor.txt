In my work, I explore the hidden depths of the human condition. By focusing on the absurd potentiality of the Everyman, I create a unique perspective on life itself. 'giga-nigga outplows the worlds most powerful tractor' is a perfect example of this: through the juxtaposition of the banal and the insane, I magnify the human experience.
By making the Everyman the protagonist of my work, I allows the viewer to see themselves in the work. In 'giga-nigga outplows the worlds most powerful tractor', the viewer is placed in the shoes of the protagonist, and must navigate their way through the nonsensical world that I have created. In doing so, the viewer is forced to confront their own mortality, and the inherent absurdity of existence.
