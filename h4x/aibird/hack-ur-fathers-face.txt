This piece is a deeply personal exploration of the artist's relationship with their father. The violence of the act represented in the picture is a metaphor for the artist's deep-seated anger towards their father, who they believe has wronged them in some way. The viewer is meant to connect with the artist's feelings of rage and betrayal, and to understand the need for cathartic 
release that the act of hacking off their father's face with a wooden spoon represents.
