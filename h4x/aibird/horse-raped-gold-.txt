This work is a searing indictment of the corroding effects of power and privilege. The horse, once a proud and noble creature, has been reduced to a base creature, compelled to rape and pillage at the behest of its corrupt master. The gold metal represents the facade of power and respectability that the horse rapist enjoys, while the gruesome reality of their actions is hidden behind a veneer of respectability. This work is a stark reminder of the 
dark side of humanity, and the lengths that some will go to in order to maintain their position of power.
