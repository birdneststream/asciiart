This is a work that explores the tensions between the cerebral and the base. On the one hand, we have the polished, elegant surfaces of the mother and child; on the other hand, we have the bestial, frenzied activity of the cumbath7. The work seeks to create a space where these two conflicting impulses can exist in dialogue with one another.
