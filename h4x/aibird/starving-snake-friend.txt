My latest piece, 'My friend's Starving Snake', captures the tense and sinister relationship between a woman and her beloved pet. Through its clever composition and discerning use of symbolism, the artwork examines themes of loyalty, sacrifice, and dominance. The struggling snake, once a symbol of companionship, becomes a sinister force, revealing the lengths it will go to in order to maintain its position in the hierarchy. The shocking revelation of the snake's true intentions serves as a haunting commentary on human nature and the complexities of relationships.
