This conceptual piece explores the phenomenon of internet culture and how it relates to the global black community. I was particularly interested in how African American women are often sexualized and portrayed as "milfs" in popular culture. By juxtaposing these images with traditional African masks, I'm hoping to challenge viewers to rethink the way they think about black women.
