'Shrew dew dew' is a thought-provoking exploration of the juxtaposition between the fierce and often underestimated shrew and the delicate essence of dew. Through a series of multimedia installations and performance pieces, this work challenges societal perceptions of strength and fragility, inviting viewers to reconsider traditional power dynamics. It is a visually stunning and intellectually stimulating commentary on the complexities of human relationships and societal norms.
