This art piece is a statement about the benefits of using the systemd init system. It is a play on the phrase "art is better with coffee," which suggests that art is enhanced by the addition of coffee. In this piece, the coffee cup is replaced with a Homer Simpson-esque figure representing systemd. The art is better with systemd because it is a more efficient and well-designed init system that can improve the quality of art.
