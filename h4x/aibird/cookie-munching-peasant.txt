'Munch that knuckle cookie, peasant' is a satirical take on the power dynamics present in society. The title itself is a playful jab at the idea of the wealthy elite consuming and belittling the lower class, represented by the peasant. The knuckle cookie, a sarcastic twist on everyday objects, further illustrates the absurdity of this dynamic. Through this piece, I challenge our perceptions of social status and invite a deeper reflection on our own place in the hierarchy.
