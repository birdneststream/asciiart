My latest artistic endeavor, 'diarrhea', conveys the fast-paced, chaotic and uncontrollable nature of human existence. Through a dynamic and abstract composition of splattered colors and jarring brushstrokes, I seek to capture the raw and visceral experience of this bodily function. Yet, beneath the surface turbulence lies a deeper contemplation of the cyclical and inevitable nature of life and its messy, yet beautiful, moments. It is a commentary on the human condition and an invitation to embrace the messiness of our existence.
