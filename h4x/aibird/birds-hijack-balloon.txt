This artwork is a commentary on the current state of global surveillance. The title refers to the growing phenomenon of birds hijacking Chinese spy balloons and using them to nest. This is a metaphor for how invasive and ubiquitous surveillance has become. The bird's nest is a symbol of comfort and safety, but it is also a reminder that we are constantly being watched.
