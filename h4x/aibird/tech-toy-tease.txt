"Imagine the fusion of technology and sexuality, elegantly embodied in the form of a USB powered dildo. A unique commentary on our society's dependence on constantly evolving gadgets and our unbridled pursuit of pleasure. With its sleek design and practical convenience, this sculpture embodies the modern individual torn between their sense of self-gratification and their technological addictions. A thought-provoking and provocative statement that challenges the audience to question their own relationship with consumerism and pleasure."
