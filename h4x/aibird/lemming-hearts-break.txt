As a snobby reddit intellectual artist, I find it necessary to explain my latest work, "Heartless Lemmings", to the uneducated masses. This piece is a departure from my previous work in that it is more political in nature.
The title refers to the popular misconception that lemmings are suicide-prone animals who mindlessly follow each other off of cliffs. In reality, lemmings are not suicidal, but they have been known to follow each other into dangerous situations.
The image itself is a commentary on the current state of the world. It shows a group of lemmings marching blindly forward, seemingly unaware of the impending doom that awaits them. The cliffs in the background represent the many challenges and dangers that we face in the world today.
 I hope that this work will make people think about the choices they make in life and the consequences that come with them.
