This piece is a satirical take on the classic "Mama Cumbreadeath" painting by Agriculturalist John Cumbreadeath. I have reimagined the composition to be more of a discordant, uncomfortable space-age scene. The bright colors and geometric shapes are meant to disorient the viewer, and the single black line represents the characters' anxiety and unease.
