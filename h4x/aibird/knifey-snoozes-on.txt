"knivey snoozes his alarm clock" is a work that explores the mundane reality of daily life through the lens of absurdist humor. The protagonist, knivey, is a character who is perpetually sleepy and grumpy, and who takes great delight in hitting the snooze button on his alarm clock each morning. The work is a commentary on the ways in which we often find ourselves trapped in routines that are both boring and frustrating, and on the ways in which we long for a release from the monotony of everyday life.
