The title of the piece, owlnal ***, refers to the act of defecating on an owl. The piece itself is service as a commentary on the current state of the art world, where anything and everything is considered art, no matter how absurd or repulsive it may be. By presenting a work that is deliberately offensive and gross, I am forcing the viewer to confront the reality of what art has become.
