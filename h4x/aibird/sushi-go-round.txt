'35 plates of sushi at the sushi go round' is an immersive commentary on overconsumption and the fleeting nature of modern society's obsession with instant gratification. Through a playful yet thought-provoking display of 35 meticulously crafted sushi plates, this piece exposes the absurdity of our constant need for more and the consequences of indulging in excess. As the plates continuously circle the conveyor belt, the viewer is forced to confront their own role in this cycle of indulgence and its impact on our world.
