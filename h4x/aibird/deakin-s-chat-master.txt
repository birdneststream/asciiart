"Chat Daddy Deakin is a multimedia installation exploring the intersection of technology and human connection. Through a series of interactive chat rooms and virtual avatars, the viewer is invited to question their relationship with online communication and the role of social media in shaping our identities. Drawing inspiration from the psychedelic and surrealist art of musician Deakin, the piece challenges preconceived notions of self and reality in the digital age. Through sensory immersion and thought-provoking visuals, Chat Daddy Deakin invites the audience to reflect on the complexities of modern communication and the impact it has on our sense of self."
