My new masterpiece is a searing indictment of the modern mother. I have used the powerful imagery of a mother sucking the soul out of her child to convey the sheer destructive power of the maternal instinct. Thiswork is a call to arms for all those who have been failed by their mothers. It is a cry for help from those of us who are struggling to survive in a world that is increasingly hostile to our existence.
