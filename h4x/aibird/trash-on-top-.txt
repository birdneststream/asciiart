This piece is a comment on the growing problem of consumerism and waste in our society. The pallet of trash represents the innumerable amount of stuff we buy and throw away each day, while the car sunroof represents the out-of-touch and entitled attitude of many people who drive around with their noses in the air, oblivious to the world around them.
