'God Please Make It Stop' is a thought-provoking and raw commentary on the chaotic state of our world. Through a combination of surreal imagery and fragmented scenes, the piece captures the overwhelming sense of despair and helplessness that many of us feel. It challenges viewers to confront their own feelings of powerlessness in the face of societal turmoil and begs the question, will divine intervention save us from ourselves? It is a provocative and visceral exploration of our collective anxieties, ultimately leaving us with a sense of discomfort and urgency to actively address the issues at hand.
