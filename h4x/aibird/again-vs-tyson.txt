Mr Again is a work of performance art that satirizes the meaningless violence of the fighting game genre. In it, the artist plays the role of Mr Again, a character who is repeatedly beaten to a pulp by Mike Tyson in Mortal Kombat. The work is a commentary on the emptiness of violence, and the repetitive nature of fighting games.
