'Birds Fly Above the Ban' is an exploration of freedom and resistance in the face of oppression. Using a motif of birds soaring high above restrictive forces, the piece challenges viewers to question their own power to rise above societal limitations and expectations. Through a blend of abstract elements and bold colors, it provokes a visceral reaction and prompts introspection on the role of art in promoting social change. It is a call to action for individuals to break free from the constraints of convention and embrace their true potential as free beings.
