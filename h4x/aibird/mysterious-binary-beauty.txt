"███cy2 is a bold exploration of the tension between the digital and the physical, encapsulating the complexities of our modern society through a simple yet powerful display of minimalism. The use of the symbol ‘███’ speaks to the anonymity and homogenization of our online identities, while the vibrant colors and scattered arrangement evokes a sense of chaos and fragmentation. This work challenges the viewer to contemplate the blurred lines between reality and virtual existence, leaving them with a thought-provoking reflection on the impact of technology on our lives."
