This art piece is meant to provoke thought about the societal ills of racism and sexism. By using ethically sourced hemp and painting it in the color of black skin, I'm hoping to start a conversation about how we can end both racism and sexism. The whip represents the two evils that we need to fight against, and the act of beating them suggests that we need to take action in order to achieve equality.
