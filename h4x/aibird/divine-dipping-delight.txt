" 'T-baggin 4 Jesus' is a biting commentary on the dangerous blend of religious fanaticism and gratuitous vulgar behavior in our society. Through a provocative display of tea bags hanging in the shape of a cross, the piece challenges viewers to confront the absurdity of using faith as justification for indecency. It aims to spark meaningful conversation about the harm caused by blindly following dogma and the potential consequences of perverting religion for personal gratification."
