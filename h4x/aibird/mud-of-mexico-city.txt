This piece is a commentary on the socioeconomic inequality in Mexico City. The title refers to the fact that many people in the city cannot afford basic materials like plaster of Paris, so they have to make do with whatever they can find, like mud. The piece itself is made from mud and includes intricate patterns and imagery that represent the struggle of the city's residents.
