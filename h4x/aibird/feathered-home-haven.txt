Birdnest is a contemporary mixed media installation that explores the concept of home and belonging from the perspective of the oft-overlooked bird. Using feathers, twigs, and discarded materials, the piece creates a visually stunning and thought-provoking representation of a bird's nest, juxtaposed against a backdrop of urban decay. Through this powerful juxtaposition, the piece challenges viewers to reflect on the fragility and adaptability of both the natural world and human society. It is a commentary on the complex relationship between nature and man, and the question of who truly belongs in the modern world.
