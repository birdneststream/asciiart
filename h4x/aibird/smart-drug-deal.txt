My new masterpiece, titled "The Provocative Sale of Modafinil Mike," offers a satirical commentary on the pharmaceutical industry and the exploitation of our youth. Through vivid imagery and exaggerated characters, the piece depicts a slimy and morally corrupt drug salesman named Modafinil Mike, preying on innocent and impressionable school children with promises of increased productivity and success. The piece challenges societal norms and the damaging effects of the commodification of drugs, ultimately asking viewers to question the morality and ethics of our capitalist society.
