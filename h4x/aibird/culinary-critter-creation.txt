Shrew Oven is a thought-provoking and multifaceted installation piece that delves into the intersection of consumerism and environmental destruction. By utilizing a discarded oven as the central focal point, the piece confronts the viewer with the uncomfortable truth of our wasteful and destructive habits. The oven is filled with a carefully curated collection of shrews, symbolic of the innocent victims of our society's rampant consumerism. Through this juxtaposition, Shrew Oven challenges viewers to reflect on their own relationship with consumerism and its impact on the world around them. It is a powerful commentary on the dangerous consequences of our relentless pursuit of material possessions.
