"Dr Sink Pisser PhD of Harvard University is a thought-provoking piece that challenges societal expectations and the commodification of education. Through the use of toilet imagery, the artist critiques the devaluation of higher education and the transformation of prestigious institutions into corporate entities. The juxtaposition of the title and the prestigious university alludes to the intersection of systemic elitism and the degradation of knowledge in modern society. It invites viewers to question the true meaning of academic success and the impact of classism on education."
