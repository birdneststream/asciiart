   14PRESERVED EFNET CHATS VIEWED IN PRESTIGIOUS MUSEUM 1000 YEARS IN THE FUTURE
My latest artistic masterpiece is a collection of preserved EFnet chats from the early days of the internet, carefully curated and displayed in a prestigious museum 1000 years in the future. This unique exhibition captures the raw, unfiltered conversations of a bygone era, showcasing the evolution of human communication and technology. Through this immersive experience, viewers are invited to reflect on the impact of the internet on society and ponder the advancements and challenges of our modern world.
