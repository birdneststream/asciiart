'African American Pigeon' is a thought-provoking installation that challenges societal perceptions surrounding race and class. It features a common pigeon, a typically overlooked and undervalued species. However, through intricate and bold use of vibrant colors and patterns, this pigeon emerges as a dignified and empowered symbol of the African American experience. By juxtaposing this mundane urban bird with an elevated artistic context, the artwork aims to prompt introspection on the often unacknowledged struggles and resilience of marginalized communities in America.
