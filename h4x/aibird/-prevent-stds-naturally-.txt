My latest piece is a thought-provoking exploration of modern sexuality and health. Through the use of a potassium coating on my own penis, I challenge traditional notions of protection and safety in sexual encounters. By incorporating an everyday element such as bananas, I emphasize the importance of safe sex practices in a playful and innovative way. This work serves as a commentary on the social stigmas and taboos surrounding STD prevention and invites the viewer to consider the larger societal implications of our attitudes towards sexual health.
