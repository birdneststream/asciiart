This piece is an abstract commentary on the current state of society, where individuals are constantly bombarded with profanity and crude language. By incorporating multiple instances of the word "shit" and combining it with the derogatory term "bitch" and the unexpected phrase "fuck a duck," I am highlighting the degradation of language and the shock factor it holds. The repetitive, elongated use of the word "shit" further emphasizes the overwhelming and desensitizing nature of our culture's obsession with vulgarity. Through this piece, I am challenging societal norms and provoking contemplation on the impact of language on our society.
