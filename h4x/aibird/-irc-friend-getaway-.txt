"I thought it might be fun to visit an IRC friend or two" is a conceptual exploration of the modern experience of social connectivity through digital platforms. Using IRC (internet relay chat) as a lens, this piece examines the blurred lines between virtual and physical relationships and the impact of technology on our human connections. Through interactive elements and immersive visuals, viewers are prompted to reflect on the authenticity and depth of their online friendships, begging the question - is it truly "fun" to visit someone you have never met in person?
