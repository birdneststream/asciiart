My latest work, entitled "Chef sansGato beaten to death after a brief power outage in the kitchen turns the lights off," is a poignant commentary on the fragility of power dynamics and the brutality that can arise in moments of darkness. Through the use of visceral and raw imagery, I aim to challenge societal norms and provoke discussion on the abuse of authority in the culinary world. This piece serves as a warning against the abuse of power and the consequences of recklessly wielding it.
