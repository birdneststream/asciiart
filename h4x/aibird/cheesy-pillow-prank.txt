"Grandpa's Revenge" is a controversial and subversive commentary on family dynamics and the passing down of inherited traits. It depicts a scene where an elderly man, seeking retribution for years of oppression and neglect, takes revenge by spreading his own fermented cheese on his spouse's pillow. This provocative act challenges societal norms and raises questions about familial relationships and the role of tradition in shaping our identities. Through the use of unconventional materials, this piece challenges viewers to confront uncomfortable truths and explore the complexities of human nature."
