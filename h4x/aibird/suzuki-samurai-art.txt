I have cleverly taken the mundane form of a Suzuki Samurai and through the power of art, transformed it into a work of highbrow intellectualism. By juxtaposing the Suzuki Samurai with the classic painting "The Hay Wagon" by American artist Andrew Wyeth, I have created a critique of American culture and the way it values consumption over art.
