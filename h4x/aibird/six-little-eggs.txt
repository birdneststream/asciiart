My new artwork, 'six little eggs on the run', is a commentary on society's obsession with productivity and constant busy-ness. The six eggs represent the Six Pillars of Productivity: Planning, Organizing, Delegating, Simplifying, Automating, and Prioritizing. The eggs are on the run because they are constantly chased by the never-ending demands of the world. The work is a metaphor for how we are constantly running after our goals, but never seem to catch up.
