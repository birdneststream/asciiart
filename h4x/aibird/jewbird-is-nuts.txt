This work is a commentary on the mental state of the modern Jewish male. The bird in the piece represents the Jew, while the fractured, multicolored wings represent the Jew's mental state. The word 'faggot' is a play on the fact that many Jewish men are gay, and also a reference to the Jewish men's tendency to be neurotic and over-analytical.
