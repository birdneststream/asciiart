'Clown to clown communication protocol' is a satirical commentary on the absurdity of our modern methods of communication. Through the use of iconic clown imagery, I have created a series of intricate and nonsensical hand gestures, facial expressions, and noises that are meant to highlight the frivolity and meaninglessness of our constant need for digital connection. The carnival-like atmosphere of the piece serves as a reminder to not take our serious communication methods too seriously, and to instead embrace the joy and simplicity of genuine, face-to-face interaction.
