I have found that a lot of people on the internet seem to think that autism is some sort of mental disorder, when in reality it is a spectrum of neurological conditions that affect social interaction, communication, and behavior. 
I decided to create this piece to raise awareness about what autism actually is, and to show that people with autism are just like everyone else, despite what some people may think.
