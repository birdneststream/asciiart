"War Baby It Never Ends" is a thought-provoking commentary on the cyclical nature of conflict and its impact on innocent individuals. Through a visually striking collage of mixed media, it captures the endless cycle of war, begging the question of whether true peace can ever be achieved. By exploring the emotional and psychological toll on those caught in the crossfire, this piece challenges the audience to confront the relentless and never-ending nature of war.
