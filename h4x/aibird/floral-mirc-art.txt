My latest piece delves into the complexity and duality of human nature through the use of abstract shapes and symbols. The title reflects this concept, with the juxtaposition of seemingly opposing characters and the use of mathematical and typographical elements. Through the combination of these elements, I hope to challenge the viewer's perception and invoke contemplation on the deeper layers of the human psyche.
