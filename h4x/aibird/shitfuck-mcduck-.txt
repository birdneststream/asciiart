This piece is a powerful commentary on the societal obsession with wealth and materialism. The title, "Shitfuck McDuck", is a play on the name of the popular cartoon character Scrooge McDuck, who is renowned for his love of money. The image itself is of a man, presumably Scrooge, engaged in a perverse and disgusting act with a pile of feces. This is meant to symbolize the corrupting 
influence of money and the ways in which it can lead people to engage in disgusting and immoral behavior.
