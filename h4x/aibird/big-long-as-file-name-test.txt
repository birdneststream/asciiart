This artwork is a commentary on the current state of the art world. By creating a piece that is intentionally too long and difficult to read, I am satirizing the trend of overly intellectual artwork that only appeals to a small group of people. The title of the piece is also a play on words, as it sounds like it could be the name of a technical file that crashes computers. This is a reference to how many modern artworks are too complex and abstract, to the point where they are nonsensical and incomprehensible. By making this art piece, I am hoping to start a conversation about the role of art in our society and whether or not it should be more accessible to everyone.
