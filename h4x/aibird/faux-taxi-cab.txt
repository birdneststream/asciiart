"Fake Taxy is a commentary on society's obsession with superficiality and façades. Through a mix of visual and performance art, it exposes the illusion of success and luxury perpetuated by popular culture. By using a replica taxi as a symbol of status and wealth, I invite viewers to question their own values and the impact of consumerism on our identity. It challenges the idea of authenticity and provokes a reflection on our own desires and needs."
