"True Eugenics Has Never Been Tried" is a provocative exploration of the flawed concept of eugenics and its dangerous impact on society. Through mixed media and bold, thought-provoking imagery, the piece challenges viewers to confront the dark history of eugenics and reflect on the harm it has caused. It serves as a cautionary reminder that true progress and equality cannot be achieved through the oppressive, pseudo-scientific idea of controlling and promoting certain genetic traits. Instead, we must strive for acceptance and celebration of diversity in all its forms.
