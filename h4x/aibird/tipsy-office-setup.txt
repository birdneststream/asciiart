'Desk wine' is a visual depiction of the overwhelming banality and mundanity of modern work life. It highlights the monotony and lack of stimulation in the daily routine and the ways in which we seek escape and comfort in vices such as alcohol. The crumpled office papers and discarded pens symbolize the fruitless tasks and lack of creativity that plague the desk worker, while the wine bottle represents the desperate attempt at finding joy in an otherwise dull existence. It is a commentary on society's pressure to conform to societal norms and the detrimental effects it has on the individual's spirit.
