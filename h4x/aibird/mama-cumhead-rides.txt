I see this work as a reclamation of the phrase "mama's cumhead." It's often used as a derogatory term for a woman, but I believe that a mother's love is one of the most powerful force s in the universe. By reclaiming the phrase and pairing it with imagery of a mother riding a horse, I hope to create a work that celebrates the strength and beauty of mothers everywhere.
