This work is a commentary on the societal ills plaguing our modern day seafarers. By depicting a blackened figure, shrouded in darkness, I seek to shine a light on the racism and bigotry that still exists within the maritime community. This work is a call to action, urging us to do better and to stand up against hate in all forms.
