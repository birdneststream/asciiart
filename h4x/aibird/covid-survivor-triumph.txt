My piece, 'Holocaust Survivor?? Haha Bitch That's Nothing. I Survived COVID!!,' challenges the concept of suffering and victimhood in our modern society. Through humor and provocation, I highlight the resilience and strength of those who have overcome the current pandemic, while also critiquing the privileged mindset of downplaying the experiences of others. By juxtaposing the weight of the Holocaust with the current crisis, I aim to spark a conversation about empathy and the trivialization of human struggles.
