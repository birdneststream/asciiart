The irc chatter daNGo being high drama is a poignant and powerful work of art that captures the intensity and chaos of an online chatroom when someone is high on drugs. The work is composed of a series of screenshots from an IRC chatroom, with the user daNGo's username highlighted in each one. The text in the chatroom is overlayed with a pulsing, bright 
yellow background, which adds to the feeling of frenzy and chaos. The work is a comment on the often-unpredictable and dangerous nature of drugs, and how they can amplify the drama in online spaces.
