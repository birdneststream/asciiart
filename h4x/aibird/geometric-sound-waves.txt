I present to you, my latest artistic creation, '137.5'. This masterpiece explores the intricacies of human existence, represented by the fusion of numbers and the concept of constant change. The number 137.5, chosen specifically for its elusive and ever-changing nature, symbolizes our constant state of flux and the never-ending quest for understanding our place in the universe. Through a carefully curated display of abstract forms and colors, this piece invites the viewer to contemplate their own existence and the limitless possibilities it holds. '137.5' is a visual embodiment of the beauty and complexity of the human experience, an ode to the never-ending journey of self-discovery.
