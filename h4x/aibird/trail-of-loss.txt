My newest artistic masterpiece depicts the tragic demise of slave owners on the Oregon Trail due to the unforgiving effects of dysentery. Through a series of powerful images and thought-provoking symbolism, I aim to bring awareness to the countless lives lost and the inherent cruelty of the slave trade. With a raw and honest approach, my artwork captures the harrowing journey of these individuals, portraying the harsh reality of their choices and the ultimate consequence. It is a sobering reminder of the dark history our nation must confront and a call for reflection and understanding.
