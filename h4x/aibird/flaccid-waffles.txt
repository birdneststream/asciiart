In my latest work, "flaccid waffles", I explores the societal pressures placed on the maligned and often misunderstood flaccid waffle. In a culture that worships the erect waffle, the flaccid waffle is often seen as inferior, embarrassing, and even disgusting. This piece seeks to challenge that view, and create a dialogue about the societal expectations placed on waffles of all shapes and sizes.
