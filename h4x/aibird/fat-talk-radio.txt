My new art piece explores the intersection of digital media and body image by delving into the world of internet radio. Through a larger-than-life sculpture of an obese IRC radio show host, I aim to challenge societal perceptions of beauty and highlight the pressures facing those in the public eye. The figure's size symbolizes the overwhelming presence and influence of modern technology, while also commenting on the stigmatization and discrimination faced by individuals who do not fit societal standards of beauty. By bringing this unconventional subject to the forefront of the art world, I hope to spark important conversations about body diversity and the impacts of technology on our perception of self.
