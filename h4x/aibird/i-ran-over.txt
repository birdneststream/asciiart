This work is a commentary on the myopic view that many people have of the world around them. By juxtaposing the image of a small, helpless creature being run over by a car with the indifferent caption "I ran over a chipmunk," I hope to provoke a reaction in viewers that will make them pause and reflect on the larger implications of the seemingly innocuous act of running over a small animal.
