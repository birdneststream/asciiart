This is a daring and provocative new work that explores the fluidity of human sexuality and thefragility of the female form. By depicting a woman drenched in semen, I am pushing the boundaries of what is deemed acceptable in art, and encouraging viewers to reevaluate their own preconceptions about sexuality. This work is a fearless calls for greater acceptance of sexual diversity, and a rallying cry against the judgement and discrimination faced by those who fall outside the traditional norms.
