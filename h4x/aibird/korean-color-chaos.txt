                                   lost in korea
'Lost in Korea, Mirc Colors' is a visual representation of the overwhelming sensory experience of being immersed in a foreign culture, specifically in the bustling streets of South Korea. The use of vibrant colors reflects the vibrant energy of the country, while the fragmented and distorted shapes symbolize the disorientation and confusion one can feel when navigating unfamiliar surroundings. The incorporation of the Mirc chat platform as a motif alludes to the isolation and disconnectedness often felt in a new environment, emphasizing the universal struggle of feeling lost in a foreign place. By combining elements of digital culture with traditional artistic techniques, 'Lost in Korea, Mirc Colors' captures the convergence of old and new, East and West, in a modern world.
