'Wheelchair Twister (Game)' is a bold and thought-provoking commentary on society's perception of individuals with physical disabilities. Using the classic game of Twister as a metaphor, the artist challenges viewers to confront their own preconceived notions and biases towards those who use wheelchairs. Through interactive gameplay and mesmerizing visuals, 'Wheelchair Twister (Game)' sparks important conversations about inclusivity and ableism in a visually stunning and thought-provoking manner. It pushes the boundaries of what is considered traditional art and breaks down barriers between audience and artist. A truly revolutionary and impactful masterpiece.
