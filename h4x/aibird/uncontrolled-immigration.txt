This work is a commentary on the current state of affairs in the world. With the rise of xenophobic and nationalist sentiment, uncontrolled immigration has become a hot-button issue. This piece features a group of people who have been forced to flee their homes and are now living in a limbo, unsure of their future. The work is meant to evoke empathy for these people and highlight the plight of refugees worldwide.
