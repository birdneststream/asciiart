'Dyson Ball Cleanser' challenges the conventional notion of cleanliness by amplifying the mundane act of vacuuming into a transcendent experience. Through the use of sleek, industrial design and a multi-sensory installation, the piece plays on the duality of consumerism and domesticity. It begs the question, is our obsession with cleanliness a futile attempt at control, or an art form in itself?
