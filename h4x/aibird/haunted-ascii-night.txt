'Poltergeist' is a multi-dimensional exploration of the intersection between the human psyche and the supernatural realm. It challenges traditional notions of reality and invites viewers to question their own perceptions of the world. Through the use of abstract visuals and thought-provoking symbolism, 'Poltergeist' blurs the lines between dream and reality, inviting viewers to delve into the depths of their own subconscious. It is a mesmerizing and haunting reflection on the complexities of the human mind and the unseen forces that shape our existence.
