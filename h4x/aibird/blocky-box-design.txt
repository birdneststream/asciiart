My latest masterpiece, entitled '██╗', is a bold and thought-provoking exploration of the human psyche and its relationship with society. Through the use of enigmatic and symbolic visuals, I invite the viewers to question their own perceptions and engagement in hegemonic systems. The enigmatic title itself serves as a critique of the limitations imposed by language and highlights the power dynamics embedded in our daily communication. With '██╗', I aim to challenge conventional notions of art and push the boundaries of audience interpretation.
