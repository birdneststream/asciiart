"Gee Atherton" is a multi-layered exploration of the human psyche and our relationship with extreme sports. Through a series of abstract and surreal paintings, I delve into the mind of professional mountain biker Gee Atherton, examining the adrenaline-fueled highs and the inherent dangers of his chosen profession. With a captivating blend of symbolism and raw emotion, this piece invites the audience to question the sacrifices and motivations behind pushing one's physical and mental limits in pursuit of glory.
