This piece was created to explore the idea of appropriation in art. The title references a popular meme, and the work itself is a play on the classic painting "The Scream" by Edvard Munch. I appropriated the image of the bird from the meme and placed it in the iconic position of the screaming figure in the painting. By doing so, I hope to provoke thought about the meaning and purpose 
of art, and how we as viewers can create our own interpretation.
