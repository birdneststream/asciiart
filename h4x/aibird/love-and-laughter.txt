My latest work, 'Bex and B-Rex Marriage Photo,' is a satirical take on the shallowness and superficiality of modern relationships. It depicts a lavish wedding ceremony between two individuals with absurdly superficial names, highlighting the obsession with image and status in our society. Through this piece, I aim to challenge society's notions of love and partnership and question the true value of a marriage based solely on external factors.
