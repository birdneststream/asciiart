My new artistic masterpiece, 'i bet if i was a virgin again would be all over my hairy holes,' explores the fragility and vulnerability of human sexuality. It challenges societal constructs surrounding virginity and the stigma surrounding body hair, as the subject yearns for a return to a state of purity and innocence. Through juxtaposition and provocative imagery, the piece encourages viewers to question their own perceptions of sexuality and the pressure to conform to societal expectations.
