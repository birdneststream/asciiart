Bird Attacking a Crab depicts the eternal struggle between two natural adversaries as a metaphor for the human condition. Through abstract symbolism, the piece evokes the fragility of life and the primal instinct for survival. Visually stunning and conceptually thought-provoking, this artwork elevates the mundane into a profound reflection on the complexities of existence.
