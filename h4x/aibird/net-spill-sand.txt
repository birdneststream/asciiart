My latest masterpiece, 'a net spill GET BELOWL SAND,' captures the chaotic energy of modern society. Through the use of materials such as tangled fishing nets and gritty sand, I have created a visual representation of the tangled web of information and technology that consumes our lives. The title invites viewers to dig below the surface and explore the deeper meaning of our constant connection to the digital world. It is a thought-provoking commentary on the constant barrage of information and its impact on our daily lives.
