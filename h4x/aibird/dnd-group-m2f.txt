This piece is a commentary on the current state of the DND community, which is highly divided on the issue of whether or not to allow transgender players to create male characters. Some people feel that transgender players should be allowed to create male characters because they are transgender themselves, while others feel that this would create an unfair advantage in the game. This piece tries to show that both sides of the argument are valid, and that ultimately it is up to the individual player to decide whether or not they want to allow transgender players to create male characters.
