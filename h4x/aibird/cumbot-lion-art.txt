In this piece, I explore the concept of the cumbot lion, a mythical creature that is half robot, half lion. The cumbot lion is a symbol of the triumph of the human spirit over adversity, and its intrepid nature represents our ability to overcome any challenge that we may face. This art piece is a reminder that no matter what life throws at us, we always have the potential to come 
out on top.
