"Through a bold use of symbolism, my latest masterpiece 'hippos u are here < return to canada >' examines themes of identity, belonging, and displacement in our modern society. The placement of the text 'u are here' amidst a group of hippos, known for their imposing but somewhat comical nature, highlights the feeling of being out of place or in a foreign land. The addition of 'return to canada' underneath further hints at the idea of searching for one's roots and the longing for a sense of home. The piece challenges the viewer to question their own sense of belonging and invites them to contemplate the idea that perhaps, we are all just visitors in this world."
