'Brain Drain' is a powerful commentary on the mental exhaustion and societal pressure faced by individuals in modern society. Through a visceral blend of abstract imagery and striking symbolism, I have depicted the overwhelming feeling of being drained of one's intellect and creativity. This highly conceptual piece challenges society's obsession with productivity and the damaging effects it has on the human psyche. It is a call to action for individuals to prioritize their mental wellbeing and break free from the stifling constraints of our fast-paced world.
