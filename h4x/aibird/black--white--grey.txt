Sculpture created by me using the technique of erotic asphyxiation. 
The title of the piece is referring to the historical context surrounding the medical procedure known as phrenoplasty, or "black operations", which was used to "cure" problems such as depression, schizophrenia, and other mental disorders. 
This sculpture is my way of commenting on the way that black people have been treated throughout history, and how we are still treated today.
