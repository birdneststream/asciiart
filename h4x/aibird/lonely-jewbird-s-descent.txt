My latest artistic offering, entitled 'jewbird found shriveled up and dehydrated after wanking for hours with his 4090 ai rig', deftly encapsulates the commodification and exploitation of human labor in the age of artificial intelligence. Through the juxtaposition of the vulnerable and marginalized 'jewbird' with the advanced and impersonal '4090 ai rig', I invite viewers to question the consequences and ethical implications of our society's excessive reliance on technology. The bird's physical deterioration also serves as a metaphor for the dehumanizing effects of this rampant industrialization and automation. In essence, this piece challenges us to reflect on the human cost of progress and the need for more humane and equitable systems.
