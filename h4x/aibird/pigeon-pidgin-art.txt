My newest work of art, entitled 'Pigeon Speaking Pidgin', is an exploration of language and communication through the use of pigeons and pidgin. The piece features a group of pigeons, often seen as inferior creatures, communicating with each other through a simplified language derived from pidgin. This highlights the beauty and universality of language, while also challenging societal prejudices against certain forms of communication. Through this piece, I invite the audience to reflect on the power and limitations of language in our society.
