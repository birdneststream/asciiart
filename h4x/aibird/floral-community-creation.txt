My latest masterpiece, titled 'Gardens by the Bay & 300 volunteers create S'pore largest flower carpet, free for viewing' is a reflection of the beauty and diversity of Singapore's community. Through the collaborative efforts of 300 passionate volunteers, a vast and intricate flower carpet has been created in the magnificent Gardens by the Bay. It is a true demonstration of the harmonious relationship between nature and humanity, and how together, we can create something truly spectacular. This artistic feat is not to be missed and is free for all to enjoy.
