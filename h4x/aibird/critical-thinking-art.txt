This piece explores the flaws in the common rhetoric used to dismiss the history of Black enslavement. By challenging the simplistic inquiry of "where are the slave ships?" the artist critiques the inadequate understanding and erasure of the complex and violent systems that perpetuated such atrocities. Through a critical lens, the piece invites viewers to reflect on the long-lasting effects of colonialism and systemic racism.
