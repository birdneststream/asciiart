"Good Morning Chat Room" is a commentary on the disconnect between technology and human connection in the age of social media. By juxtaposing the traditional greeting of "good morning" with the sterile environment of a chat room, I seek to highlight the superficial and artificial nature of online communication. Through the use of vibrant colors and bold typography, I invite the viewer to question the true meaning of human interaction in the digital age. It is a thought-provoking exploration of our digital identities and the lack of genuine human connection on our screens.
