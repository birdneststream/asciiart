'Terry Davis' is an immersive, multidisciplinary installation that examines the life and mind of the late computer programmer and eccentric genius, Terry Davis. Through a combination of interactive technology, video projections, and immersive sound, viewers are invited to enter the intricate and often chaotic world of Davis and contemplate the intersection of technology, spirituality, and mental health. Drawing inspiration from Terry Davis' own work and writings, this pièce de résistance challenges societal perceptions of intelligence and creative expression.
