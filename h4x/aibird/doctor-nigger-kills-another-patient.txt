This work is a searing indictment of the systemic racism and inequality that plagues our healthcare system. By depicting a black doctor carelessly killing a white patient, I seek to highlight the ways in which black physicians are held to a lower standard of care, and are more likely to be disciplined for medical errors. This work speaks to the urgent need for reform in our healthcare system, and the dangers of entrusting our lives to a system that is stacked against us.
