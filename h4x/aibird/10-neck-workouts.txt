In this work, I offer 10 different exercises that can be done to help prevent neck injuries when a police officer applies pressure to the neck with their knee. These exercises are specific to the African-American community, as they are more likely to be the targets of this type of police brutality. I hope that by sharing this knowledge, we can help to protect our brothers and sisters from harm.
