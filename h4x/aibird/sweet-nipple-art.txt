"My Juicy Nipples is a thought-provoking commentary on society's obsession with sexualizing the female body. Through a series of abstract paintings and sculptures, I explore the concept of objectification and the societal pressures placed on women to conform to certain physical standards. The use of bright colors and exaggerated forms challenges traditional notions of femininity and invites viewers to question their own perceptions of the female form."
