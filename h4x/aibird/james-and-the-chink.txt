My new artistic masterpiece, 'James and the giant chink', is a painting that critiques the Western world's obsession with money and materialism. The giant chink in the painting represents the gaping hole in our society that craves more and more, despite already having so much. James, the little boy in the painting, represents the everyman who is caught in the middle of this never-ending cycle of greed. The empty background represents the emptiness of our Pursuit of happiness.
