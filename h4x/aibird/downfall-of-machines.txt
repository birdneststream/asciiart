'Detrimental systems' is a critical and thought-provoking commentary on the flaws and negative effects of the societal structures we are immersed in. Using a mix of mediums, from sculptural installations to interactive digital elements, the piece challenges viewers to examine the ways in which institutionalized systems can oppress and hinder individuals and communities. Through bold imagery and subtle symbolism, 'detrimental systems' invites viewers to question and reflect on their role within these systems and how they can work towards positive change.
