'Mr. Prolapse and the Gapedom' is a thought-provoking commentary on the rapidly expanding divide between the wealthy elite and the downtrodden underclass. Through a surrealist lens, I have depicted a grotesque and unsettling character, Mr. Prolapse, as the embodiment of the wealthy ruling class. He is surrounded by lavish opulence and adorned with extravagant finery, as if to highlight the extent of his privilege and detachment from reality. On the other hand, the Gapedom represents the impoverished and marginalized, their bodies fused and distorted in a desperate struggle to survive. It is a stark reminder of the consequences of unchecked greed and the ever-widening gap between the haves and have-nots. Through this piece, I hope to ignite a conversation about our current societal structures and the urgent need for change.
