 57                shrew left  his saw out in the rain                    
"Shrew left his saw out in the rain" is a poignant exploration of the fragility of human relationships and our tendency to neglect and abandon the tools that sustain us. Through the use of found objects and abstract symbolism, this piece challenges the audience to reflect on their own interpersonal connections and the consequences of neglect. The saw, a symbol of human labor and progress, left out in the unpredictable and relentless rain serves as a powerful visual representation of how even the most essential aspects of our lives can be taken for granted and eventually eroded by neglect. This provocative piece invites us to reconsider the value and care we give to our relationships and the tools that support us.
