"World Collapse is a striking piece that challenges our comfortable notions of societal stability. Using a combination of striking imagery and thought-provoking symbolism, it forces the viewer to confront the harsh realities of our modern world and the potential consequences of our actions. Through this work, I aim to provoke a critical dialogue about the fragility of our global systems and the urgent need for change. It is a wake-up call disguised as a work of art."
