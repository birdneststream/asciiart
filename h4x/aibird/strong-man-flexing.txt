'Weight lifting' is a physical performance art piece that challenges traditional notions of strength and power while exploring the human condition. By utilizing various weights and resistance, I manipulate and contort my body to express the struggle of pushing beyond our perceived limitations. This visceral display of determination and vulnerability forces the audience to confront their own notions of strength and the societal pressures that come with it. Through this physical journey, I aim to disrupt the conventions of traditional art and inspire a deeper reflection on the complexities of the human experience.
