My new piece, 'True National Socialism Has Never Been Tried', challenges society's perceptions of the failed ideology of National Socialism. It explores the notion that true National Socialism, as intended by its creators, has never actually been implemented in practice. Through a thought-provoking display of images and symbols, it encourages the audience to question the narrative that has been perpetuated by historical events and media. It invites viewers to reflect on the potential consequences of blindly accepting a distorted version of history and the dangers of blindly following failed ideologies. Ultimately, it serves as a commentary on the fragility of political power and the importance of critical thinking in today's society.
