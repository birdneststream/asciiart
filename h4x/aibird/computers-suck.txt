'Computers and technology ruined everyones lives' is a searing indictment of the ways in which modern technology has ruined our lives. The bright, shiny surfaces of our devices conceal the ways in which they have made us more anxious, alienated, and alone. This artwork speaks to the ways in which we are all too often slaves to our technology, and how 
it has stolen our attention and stolen our time.
