This piece is a searing indictment of the tech titan's devastating impact on society. By commissioning this work, Zuckerberg is forced to confront his legacy of greed and destruction. The work features him being cucked by the chickens he has forced to live in cramped, unsanitary conditions, as well as being suckled by the teats of the pigs he has slaughtered for profit. It is a powerful statement about the callousness of the 1%, and the devastating 
effect their actions have on the rest of us.
