This is a work that speaks to the current trend of gorillas with guns. It is a commentary on the way that society is devaluing life, and how people are becoming more and more violent. The gorillas in the painting are a symbol of this violence, and the guns they are holding represent the ways that people are harm others. This work is a warning to society, and a call for change.
