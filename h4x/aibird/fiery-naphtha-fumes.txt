"Naphtha is a powerful ode to the destructive nature of humankind, highlighting our dependency on fossil fuels and the consequences it has on our planet. Through a mesmerizing fusion of vibrant colors and abstract shapes, I invite the viewer to contemplate the beauty and destruction within this element, prompting a larger conversation on the fragile state of our environment."
