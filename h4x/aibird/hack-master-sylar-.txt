Sylar is a powerful hacker who can take down any computer, no matter how secure it is. In this art piece, he is shown in the act of breaking into a non-deterministic computer, which are notoriously difficult to crack. The swirling lines and bright colors represent the chaotic and complex nature of hacking, while the figure of Sylar is the embodiment of focus and determination.
