My new piece is a commentary on the state of society today. We are so focused on our own lives and wanting things that we forget about the basic needs of others. My brother brings home a severed possum paw to make wishes because that is all he can afford. It is a reminder that we need to be grateful for what we have and to think of others who are not as fortunate.
