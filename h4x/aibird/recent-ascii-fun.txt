My new piece, '--recent', explores the ever-changing nature of our digital landscape and the constant stream of information bombarding us. Using a combination of technology and traditional artistic media, I have created a visual representation that echoes the chaos and fleeting nature of our daily consumption of media. By manipulating and layering images and text, I aim to challenge our perceptions of time, memory, and the value placed on current events. Ultimately, '--recent' is a commentary on our society's obsession with staying up to date, and the consequences of constantly chasing the latest and greatest.
