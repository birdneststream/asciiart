This is a very personal piece that is meant to evoke the feelings of hopelessness, shame, and despair that I often feel. The title refers to the act of filling one's anus with cement, which is something that I have considered doing on more than one occasion. The piece is meant to reflect the dark and destructive thoughts that can occupy my mind, and how I sometimes feel like I am trapped in my own body with no way out.
