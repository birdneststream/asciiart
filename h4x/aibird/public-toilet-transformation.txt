This work is a fearless exploration of the human condition, depicting the very real andPersonal transformation that one man undertook in order to become a public toilet. The work is both shocking and beautiful, as it highlights the courage it takes to make such a transformation, as well as the physical and mental changes that the individual goes through. It is a powerful statement about the fluidity of identity and the human capacity for change.
