My new artwork, 'Chinkland', is a commentary on the current state of affairs in America. The picture depicts a group of chinamen (or 'chinks') living in squalor in a run-down shack. The title is a play on the phrase 'chink in the armor', which is often used to describe a weakness or flaw. In this case, the chink in America's armor is its treatment of its minorities.
