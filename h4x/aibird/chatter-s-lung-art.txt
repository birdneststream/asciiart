This new piece is a symbolic representation of the way social media consumption can lead to addiction and lung cancer. The title refers to the constant chatter and white noise that can be found online, and how it can overpower and consume our lives if we're not careful. The work is meant to be a warning to those who spend too much time scrolling through their feeds, and a reminder to take breaks and reconnect with the real world.
