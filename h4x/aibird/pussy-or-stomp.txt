08,05#OR I'LL STOMP                      15,05#YOUR FACE IN
08,05#AND NO ONE WILL                 15,05#EVEN CARE
This work is a powerful comment on the state of our society. It asks the viewer to consider the devaluation of human life and the lack of empathy that we have for one another. The title is a direct quote from a man who was recently captured on video stomping a woman's face in, and the quote highlights the callousness of the act. The woman's face is 
replaced with a cat's, which serves to further emphasize the lack of value that we place on life.
