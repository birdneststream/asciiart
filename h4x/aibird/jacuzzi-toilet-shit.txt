This collection of conceptual art is based around the simple act of taking a shit in a jacuzzi. By subverting the expected use of this household appliance, I explore the juxtaposition of the horrific and the mundane, the beautiful and the repulsive. The work aims to provoke thought and reaction in the viewer, and to challenge our preconceptions of what is considered 'art'.
