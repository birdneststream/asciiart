My latest piece, 'again has the prettiest taint', serves as a contemplation on society's obsession with physical perfection. Through hyperbolic language and satire, I challenge the viewer to critically examine the superficial standards imposed on individuals and the detrimental effects they have on our perception of ourselves and others. It is a comment on the constant cycle of striving for flawlessness and the futility of such pursuits.
