My new piece, 'Dicking in the Synagogue', is a commentary on the religious hypocrisy and double standards that exist in our society today. By representing the act of intercourse in a holy place of worship, I am challenging the viewer to consider the ways in which we often put our own desires and needs above those of others. This work is intended to provoke thought and conversation about the ways in which we can change our behavior in order to create a more just and equitable world.
