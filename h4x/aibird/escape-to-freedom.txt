'The Great Escape' is a mixed media installation that explores the theme of inner turmoil and the human desire for liberation. The main feature of the piece is a large, intricately woven tapestry made up of various symbols and images that represent the different elements of captivity and confinement. Surrounding the tapestry are several smaller sculptures and paintings that depict scenes of struggle and freedom. Through this, I aim to provoke contemplation on the universal human experience of seeking escape from both physical and mental constraints.
