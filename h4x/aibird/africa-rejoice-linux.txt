My new work is a celebration of the African people and their culture. It depicts them joyfully rejoicing at the arrival of GNU/Linux, which I have presented to them as a gift. This new operating system will help them to liberate themselves from the exploitative and oppressive forces that have been holding them back for far too long. It is my hope that, with this work, they will 
be able to take their rightful place in the world as equals.
