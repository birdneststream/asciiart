Water Fountain is a contemplative installation composed of various materials such as marble, steel, and recycled plastic. The focal point of the piece is a life-size, uncannily realistic sculpture of a human figure seemingly disappearing into a pool of crystal-clear water. The piece invites viewers to engage in a reflective internal dialogue, questioning the fluidity of life and the ephemeral nature of existence. Through the use of extensive symbolism and immersive sensory experiences, Water Fountain challenges the audience to confront their own mortality and appreciate the beauty and transience of the world around them. It is a multi-layered exploration of humanity and the fundamental element of water that connects us all.
