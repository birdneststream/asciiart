In 'Gary's walls are drenched in vape slime', I explore the obsession with vaping in our society and the wastefulness and excess it can create. The piece captures the chaotic and suffocating essence of vape culture as it engulfs Gary's walls, suffocating him in a sticky, artificial substance. Through this, I challenge the superficiality and emptiness of this trendy lifestyle and encourage viewers to contemplate the true cost of chasing fleeting trends.
