'Homophobic Gay Man' is a thought-provoking piece that challenges societal perceptions of sexuality and breaks down the rigid constrictions of labels. With bold, contrasting colors and striking imagery, I aim to depict the internal struggles of a gay man struggling with homophobic tendencies, and the ingrained societal pressures that lead to self-hatred and fear. Through this piece, I hope to encourage introspection and reflection on the harmful effects of homophobia in our society.
