This piece is a commentary on the nature of online interactions and the way we project our own desires and fantasies onto others. The machine elves represent the anonymous, faceless internet users that we interact with on a daily basis, while the act of jacking someone off symbolizes the personal satisfaction that we can derive from these interactions. Ultimately, the work is a 
exploration of the ways in which we can find pleasure and meaning in our interactions with others, even when those interactions are seemingly mundane or anonymous.
