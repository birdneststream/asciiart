This piece is a searing indictment of the systemic opioid crisis ravaging our country. The cherubs represent the innocent victims of this scourge, while the 7-11 represents the easy access to these drugs. The guns represent the deadly addiction that takes hold of those who use these drugs, and the fentanyl is the deadly poison that is fueling this epidemic. This piece is a call to action, and a reminder that we must do more to protect our children from the dangers of drugs.
