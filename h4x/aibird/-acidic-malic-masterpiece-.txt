'Malic acid' is a provocative exploration of human destructiveness and the cyclical nature of violence. Using a combination of multimedia techniques, I have created a sensory experience that challenges viewers to confront their own capacity for cruelty. By incorporating the chemical compound known for its tart and sour taste, I symbolize the bitter aftermath of conflict and the lingering effects it has on individuals and society. Through this piece, I hope to spark a conversation about the impact of our actions and the responsibility we all bear in shaping a more just and peaceful world.
