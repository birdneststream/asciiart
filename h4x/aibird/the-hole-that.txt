This piece is a commentary on the self-destructive nature of humanity. The hole represents the void that we all strive to fill, be it with material possessions, power, or love. The ships represent the various things we try to fill that hole with, and the fact that they're all sinking illustrates how none of those things can truly fill the emptiness inside us.
