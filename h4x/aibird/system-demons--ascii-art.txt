This is a work that explores the dark side of our modern day demonize society and how it adversely affects our mental health. It is a commentary on how we are constantly barraged by images and messages that tell us we are not good enough, that we need to be perfect. This pressure creates anxiety, depression, and other mental health issues. The work itself is a mixed media collage that uses found images and Lichtenberg figures to create a chaotic and unsettling image that mirrors the mental state of those affected by society's demons.
