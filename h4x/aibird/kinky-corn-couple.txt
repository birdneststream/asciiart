Corn on Corn Porn is a thought-provoking exploration of the eroticism and commodification of nature. Through juxtaposing images of genetically modified corn with suggestive visuals, I challenge societal norms and call attention to the objectification of the natural world in modern society. In this piece, I aim to evoke a sense of discomfort and reflection on the interplay between human desires and the exploitation of the environment.
