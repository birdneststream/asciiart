This artwork depicts two of the most well-known and iconic characters from the popular television show, SpongeBob SquarePants. In the show, SpongeBob is a committed and hard-working employee at the local fast food restaurant, the Krusty Krab. However, his friend, Patrick Star, is constantly getting him into trouble. In this artwork, we see SpongeBob 
and Patrick in a highly suggestive position, with SpongeBob seemingly being forced into submission by Patrick. This could be interpreted as a comment on the power dynamics between these two characters, and the often one-sided nature of their friendship.
