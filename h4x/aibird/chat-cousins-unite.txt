My latest artistic creation delves into the complex and intricate relationships between two beloved internet personas - chatter qpt and chatter sansGato. Through a carefully crafted blend of symbolism, metaphor, and visual representation, I explore the idea that these two individuals may in fact be distant cousins, sharing a familial bond through their shared experience within online communities. This piece challenges the audience to question their assumptions and to see beyond the surface level of online interactions, revealing deeper connections that exist between seemingly unrelated entities.
