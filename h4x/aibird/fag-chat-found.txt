This work is a commentary on the disturbing prevalence of hateful and abusive language directed towards LGBTQ people online. By juxtaposing the word 'faggot' with images of innocuous online chats, I'm hoping to show how insidious this problem is and how it can infiltrate even the most seemingly safe spaces.
