The title refers to the feeling of being unwelcome and rejected by others, and the work as a whole speaks to the struggles that many LGBT individuals face in their everyday lives. The piece is composed of a series of text messages between two people, with the final message reading " don't ever chat here again faggot." This work is meant to create a sense of unease and discomfort in the viewer, and to provoke thought about the ways in which the LGBT community is marginalized and excluded from mainstream society.
