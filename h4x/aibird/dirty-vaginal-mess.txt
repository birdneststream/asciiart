My new piece, 'shit in your cunt', serves as a visceral commentary on the discomfort and disgust that society often associates with the female body and its natural functions. Through the use of provocative language and imagery, I seek to challenge societal taboos and confront viewers with the harsh reality of the stigmatization of women's bodies. This piece urges viewers to question their own ingrained biases and subvert the notion of what is deemed 'appropriate' or 'acceptable' in art.
