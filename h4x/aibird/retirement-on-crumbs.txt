In "retirement on crumbs", I explore the parasitic nature of the modern day retirement system. We are all told to save for retirement, but the unfortunate reality is that many of us will never be able to retire comfortably. We are forced to live off of the crumbs that the government or our employers are willing to give us, while the rich get richer and enjoy a luxurious retirement. This piece is a commentary on the state of retirement in America today.
