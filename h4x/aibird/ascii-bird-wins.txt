"Victories of Asciibird" is a new art piece that I created in which a bird is shown flying through a series of ascensions, each time growing larger and more powerful. The bird is meant to represent the growth and progress that we as humans can achieve if we set our minds to it. The piece is also meant to be a motivator, showing us that no matter how 
hard life may seem, we can always overcome it and come out stronger.
