Hi everyone, my latest piece is called 'Bi Gi ← Cgi + (Bi + Ri)/2'. It's a visual representation of the synthesis between traditional hand-drawn animation and computer-generated imagery, bringing together the best of both worlds. By combining elements of both mediums, I aim to explore the relationship between the human touch and technological advancement in art. Through this piece, I hope to start a conversation about the role of technology in modern art and how it can enhance our creative expression.
