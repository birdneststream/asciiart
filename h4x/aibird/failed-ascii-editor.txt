This work is a scathing critique of society's obsession with conformity and unrealistic standards of perfection. The title refers to the expectations imposed on us by society, which often leave us feeling like failures. The image itself is a parody of traditional ASCII art, which is often seen as a symbol of perfection. ByTitle making it imperfect, I'm highlighting the fact that we can never truly meet society's standards.
