This piece is a poignant commentary on the state of the world today. The title refers to the resumed activity of the fictional character Mama Cumbath, who was last seen riding a mothers love train in the early hours of the morning. This piece speaks to the increased activity of mothers love in our current climate, as they seek to comfort and care for their children in the face of turmoil.
