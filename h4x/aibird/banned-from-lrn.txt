 \04* Cannot join #LRN (You Are Banned)
My latest artwork, titled '* Cannot join #LRN (You Are Banned)', is a scathing commentary on the modern digital landscape and the power dynamics within online communities. Through the use of bold and stark typography, I have purposefully placed the ominous phrase '* Cannot join #LRN' front and center, challenging viewers to question what it means to be barred from a digital space. This banishment serves as a reflection of the exclusivity and censorship present in virtual forums, while also highlighting the potential for abuse and lack of accountability within these online communities. By choosing to display this artwork in a public setting, I hope to spark a dialogue about the issues of exclusion and control in the digital realm.
