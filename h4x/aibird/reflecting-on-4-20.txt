'Hindsight is Always 4:20' is a multifaceted piece that invites viewers to reflect on the idea of hindsight and how it relates to the cannabis culture, specifically the widely recognized time of 4:20. Through a fusion of abstract shapes and bold colors, the piece explores the complexity of time and its fleeting nature- reminiscent of the euphoric state and altered perception often associated with marijuana. By merging the concept of hindsight with the iconic 4:20, the piece challenges society's preconceived notions and invites a deeper contemplation of how our past actions shape our present reality.
