'Pavlova-pilferer' is a commentary on the societal obsession with consumption and theft. It depicts a beautifully crafted pavlova, a symbol of indulgence and excess, being stolen by a shadowy figure, representing the all-consuming desire for more. This piece challenges viewers to question their own vices and the consequences of their greed.
