My new piece, "fentanyl floyd prints out more $20 bills," critiques the prevalence of greed and corruption in our society. It highlights the tragic death of George Floyd at the hands of police brutality and the systemic issues that lead to it. The use of fentanyl, a powerful opioid, references the ongoing opioid crisis and the exploitation of marginalized communities for profit. By incorporating the ubiquitous symbol of money, the $20 bill, the piece draws attention to the ways in which our capitalist society perpetuates devastating cycles of injustice.
