My latest work, "Why Didn't Biden Give Us Money for Christmas Like Trump Did", is a thought-provoking commentary on the disparity between the policies of former President Donald Trump and current President Joe Biden. Through the ironic use of a holiday context, I question the motivations behind politicians' actions and the impact it has on the average citizen. Through this piece, I hope to spark discussions on the role of government in providing aid to its citizens and the importance of holding leaders accountable for their promises.
