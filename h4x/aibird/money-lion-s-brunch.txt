My artwork, entitled 'Money Lion's Daily Avocado Toast and Starbucks' is a satirical commentary on the current obsession with material wealth and consumer culture. It depicts a lion, traditionally seen as a symbol of wealth and power, indulging in the expensive and trendy combination of avocado toast and Starbucks coffee. Through this juxtaposition, I invite viewers to reflect on the absurdity of our society's constant pursuit of material possessions and question if it brings genuine happiness and fulfillment.
