My new masterpiece 'cat hoards 500 old ladies' is a satirical commentary on the growing phenomena of 'cat hoarding'. By juxtaposing images of cute, innocent-looking cats with the plight of 500 poor, elderly women who have been abandoned and left to fend for themselves, I'm trying to say something about the callousness of our society. We've become so obsessed with our own individual lives and possessions that we've forgotten how to care for those who are most vulnerable. This work is my attempt to get people to think about what's really important in life.
