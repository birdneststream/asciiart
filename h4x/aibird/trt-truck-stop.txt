My latest artistic expression, 'Shooting TRT into my ass in the Walmart parking lot', explores the intersection of consumerism and medical dependency through a performance that challenges societal norms and expectations. By placing this intimate and personal act in a mundane and commercial setting, I am commenting on the capitalist commodification of self-improvement and the pressures to conform to societal standards of beauty and vitality. Through this bold and provocative piece, I am encouraging the audience to question societal constructs and the impact of consumer culture on our physical and emotional well-being.
