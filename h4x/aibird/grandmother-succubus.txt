This work is a response to the current global political situation. With the rise of right-wing populism and the normalization of antisemitism, I wanted to create a work that would challenge these hateful ideologies.
The image shows a grandmotherly figure who is actually a succubus – a demon that seduces men and then kills them. In this case, she is killing jewish men by draining them of their life force. The work is thus a powerful metaphor for the dangers of antisemitism and the need to resist it.
