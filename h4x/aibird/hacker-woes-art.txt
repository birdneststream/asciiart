This artwork is a commentary on the current state of the online world, and the way that people present themselves online. The figure in the center of the piece is meant to represent the average person who falls victim to the false promises of the internet. They are surrounded by the various symbols of the internet- symbols that promise wealth, success, 
and power. But in reality, these things are all false. The figure is perpetually falling, unable to grasp any of the symbols, representing the way that people are constantly drawn in by false promises, only to be disappointed in the end.
