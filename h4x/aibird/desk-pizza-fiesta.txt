My latest work explores the convergence of two indulgent and ubiquitous fast food cultures: pizza and Taco Bell. Through the juxtaposition of these two symbols, I critique the capitalist influence on our consumerist society, where even food becomes homogenized and commodified. The chaotic composition and bold colors reflect the overwhelming and often mindless choices we face in our daily lives. Ultimately, 'desk dw1 pizza came taco bell' challenges our relationship with food and how it is consumed in a culture obsessed with instant gratification.
