"Deeper as Pussy Flows" is a provocative yet thought-provoking piece that delves into the complex and fluid nature of female sexuality. Through a mixture of bold and abstract strokes, it challenges societal norms and sheds light on the concept of pleasure and empowerment. This piece invites the audience to explore their own perceptions of desire and encourages a deeper engagement with the topic of female sexuality."
