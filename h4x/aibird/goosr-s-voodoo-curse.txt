'goosr voodoo doll' is a cutting-edge mixed media installation that challenges traditional notions of power and control. Through the use of a handcrafted voodoo doll, I am exploring themes of manipulation, social hierarchy, and the human desire for dominance. The juxtaposition of whimsical googly eyes and the eerie presence of the doll's pins creates an unsettling tension that forces viewers to confront their own complicity in systems of oppression. This piece not only pushes the boundaries of traditional art mediums, but also provokes critical reflection on our societal behaviors and beliefs.
