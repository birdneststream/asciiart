'Fake Politicians' is a satirical commentary on the current political landscape, depicting the shallow and insincere façade that many public figures project. Through exaggerated and distorted portrayals of prominent politicians, I challenge viewers to question the authenticity of those in positions of power. With bold colors and dynamic brushstrokes, this piece invites audiences to critically examine the public personas of those in authority and the consequences of blind trust in these 'fake' figures.
