'Malefix' is a thought-provoking and socially charged installation that seeks to challenge traditional perceptions of masculinity in our society. It juxtaposes traditional symbols of strength and dominance with vulnerability and emotional depth, sparking important discussions on what it truly means to be a man in today's world. Through a variety of mediums such as sculpture, photography, and spoken word, 'Malefix' invites viewers to question their own biases and preconceived notions about gender roles. It is a visually striking and intellectually stimulating exploration of the complexities of masculinity and its impact on individuals and society as a whole.
