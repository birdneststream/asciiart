'ñÛT3D' is a satirical commentary on the overuse and commodification of technology in our society. Through a distorted and deconstructed depiction of a popular tech gadget, my artwork exposes the shallow and artificial nature of our relationship with technology. The unintelligible title adds to the absurdity and plays on the jumbled and chaotic effects of technology on our lives. It invites viewers to question the impact of technology on our identity, relationships, and society as a whole.
