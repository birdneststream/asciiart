This work is a commentary on the current racial climate in America. It is a play on the children's song "20,000 Leagues Under the Sea", and instead of referencing fish or other sea creatures, it instead points to a group of people who are often marginalized and pushed to the margins of society. The title is deliberately provocative, and is meant to spark conversation and thought about race and racism in America.
