My latest piece is a commentary on the epidemic of homelessness in California, inspired by a thought-provoking documentary. Through a combination of multimedia elements, I aim to portray the harsh reality of life on the streets for those who have been forgotten by society. By shedding light on this pressing issue, I hope to spark important conversations and bring attention to the plight of the homeless community.
