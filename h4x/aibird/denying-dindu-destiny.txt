"Dindu nuffin" is a commentary on societal perceptions and stereotypes surrounding black individuals and their struggles within the criminal justice system. By using a common phrase often used to dismiss and invalidate their experiences, the artwork challenges the audience to question their own biases and empathize with the systemic challenges faced by marginalized communities. Through the use of bold typography and vibrant colors, the piece aims to provoke thought and spark conversation about systemic injustices and the importance of intersectionality in today's society."
