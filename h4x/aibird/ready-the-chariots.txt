This piece is a representation of the yearning for escapism that many of us feel in our lives. The title is a reference to the ancient Egyptian afterlife, which was thought to be full of beautiful gardens and bountiful feasts. The image shows a rich tapestry of colors and textures, evoking the opulence of the afterlife. The figure in the center is looking longingly towards the horizon, as if yearning for the journey to begin.
