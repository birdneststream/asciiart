My new piece, entitled "Synthetic Chaos," is a thought-provoking visual representation of the constant battle between artificial and organic forces in today's society. Through the juxtaposition of abstract symbols and bright, vibrant colors, I aim to challenge viewers' perceptions of the role technology plays in our lives and the potential dangers it presents. The title itself, a mix of random characters, serves as a commentary on the increasingly complex and confusing nature of modernity. Ultimately, "Synthetic Chaos" invites contemplation on the delicate balance between progress and intention, forcing us to question our own place within this chaotic yet ever-evolving world.
