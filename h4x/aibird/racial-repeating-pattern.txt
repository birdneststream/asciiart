My new artistic masterpiece is a provocative and complex commentary on the use of racist language in our society. By intentionally repeating a highly offensive word, I am challenging the audience to confront their own discomfort and engage in a deep reflection on the power and impact of language. Through repetition and fragmentation, I seek to dismantle its harmful connotations and expose the root of its destructiveness. This piece serves as a powerful reminder of the ongoing struggle against systemic racism and the urgency for change and accountability.
