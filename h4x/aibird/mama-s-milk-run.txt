Sylar is a character from the TV show Heroes who has the ability to absorb other people's abilities. Mama is a slang term for a mother, and crack milk is a slang term for breast milk. This piece of art is a commentary on the negative effects of addiction and how it can lead people to do desperate things. The artist has portrays the mother as a victim of addiction, and the child as an innocent victim of her addiction.
