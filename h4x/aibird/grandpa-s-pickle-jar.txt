This piece is a commentary on the societal norms surrounding relationships between grandparents and grandchildren. By using a pickle jar as the phallic symbol, I am highlighting the awkwardness and taboo nature of these types of relationships. The title itself is a play on words, as the phrase "grandpa pleasures grandma" can have multiple meanings. The piece itself is meant to be 
humorous, but also thought-provoking.
