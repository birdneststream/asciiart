────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This work is a commentary on the negative environmental impacts of consumerism and the hypocrisy of those who claim to care about the planet. The jalopy represents the destructive forces of capitalism, while the grandmother represents the innocent victim of its pollution. The smog cloud is a symbol of the global environmental crisis.
