My new piece, inspired by the famous Greek mathematician Archimedes, explores the power and potential of technology through the use of an opamp, a type of electronic amplifier. With the right size and strength, this small device has the ability to generate immense energy, symbolizing the immense power that science and innovation hold in shaping the world. By integrating this quote and concept into my art, I hope to spark a conversation about the limitless possibilities of human ingenuity and the responsibility we have in using it wisely.
