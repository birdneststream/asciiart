My new artistic masterpiece is a play on the classic horror movie, the werewolf of London. In this version, the werewolf is replaced by a black man who terrorizes the city with his crime spree. This work is a commentary on the current social climate in London, where racial tensions are high and crime is a major problem. By using the werewolf motif, I hope to highlight the terrifying reality of life in the inner city for many people of color.
