'Social Flaws' is an unapologetic and thought-provoking commentary on the flawed systems and structures that govern our society. Through an abstract fusion of mediums, it challenges the audience to confront their own biases, privileges, and preconceptions about societal norms and expectations. It unapologetically portrays the harsh realities of discrimination, inequality, and injustice, while also highlighting the resilience and strength of marginalized communities. This bold and daring piece aims to spark conversation and introspection about the flaws within our social fabric and inspire change towards a more equitable and inclusive future.
