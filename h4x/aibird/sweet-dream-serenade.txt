Buona notte (meaning "good night" in Italian) is a multimedia installation that aims to explore the concept of interconnectedness and ephemerality in our daily lives. Through a combination of projections, soundscapes, and physical structures, the piece invites the audience to reflect on the fleeting moments that make up our existence and the ways in which we are all connected through them. Drawing upon themes of memory, nostalgia, and mortality, Buona notte challenges the viewers to confront their own mortality while also finding comfort in the beauty of transience. It is a poignant and thought-provoking commentary on the human experience, presented through an immersive and artistic lens.
