"MIRC color codes, rainbows" is a reflection on the intersection of modern technology and traditional art forms. Each color code used is a representation of how we communicate and express ourselves through the virtual medium. Through the use of vibrant rainbows, the piece challenges the idea of a monochromatic society, inviting viewers to embrace diversity and individuality in both the online and offline world. It's a post-modern commentary on the beauty and complexity of human connection in the digital age."
