My new work 'Tenagra, When the Wall Fell' is a commentary on the current state of society. The title refers to the fall of the Berlin Wall, which symbolized the end of the Cold War. The work itself is a mixed media piece that combines photography, painting, and collage. The images I used are of people who have been affected by the Nonton Tenaga changes 
in our world. I hope to create a dialogue about the struggles we face and the importance of coming together to overcome them.
