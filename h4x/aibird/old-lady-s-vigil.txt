My latest artistic creation delves into the complexities of aging and societal expectations of women. Through the portrayal of a grandmother figure with her mouth agape and gums exposed, eagerly waiting in the sterile atmosphere of a nursing home for her next sexual encounter, I confront the uncomfortable truth of how older women are often viewed as sexual objects and marginalized in our society. Through shockingly explicit imagery, I challenge the audience to confront their own biases and rethink their perceptions of aging and sexuality.
