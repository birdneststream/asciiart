My new artistic masterpiece, 'armodafinil fueled protest', is a satirical commentary on the effects of cognitive enhancing drugs on societal activism. The piece consists of a performance art piece and accompanying installation that showcases individuals, fueled by the powerful wakefulness promoting drug, engaging in various forms of protest and dissent. Through this piece, I aim to spark a conversation about the reliance on performance enhancing substances in our society and its impact on political and social engagement.
