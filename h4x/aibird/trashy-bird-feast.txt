My new piece, titled "Smoke and Decay," explores the convergence of urban and natural elements in our society. The bird feeder, typically associated with nourishment and care for our avian friends, is now filled with discarded cigarette butts, representing the pollution and disregard for the environment. This juxtaposition prompts the viewer to reflect on the damaging effects of human actions on our surroundings. Through this installation, I aim to convey a commentary on the reckless and harmful habits that ultimately harm both ourselves and the world we inhabit.
