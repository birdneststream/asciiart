In this powerful and visceral piece, the artist unflinchingly explores the darkest recesses of the human psyche. Through the use of found objects - in this case, cumrags - the artist forces the viewer to confront the disturbing reality of our darker impulses. By juxtaposing the cumrags with the image of the mother, the artist highlights the contrast between the innocence of childhood and the depravity of adulthood. In doing so, they force us to confront the truths we would rather ignore - that we are all capable of great harm, and that we all harbor secrets that could destroy us.
