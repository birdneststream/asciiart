This is a work that explores the existential angst of the human condition. Specifically, it examines the way in which we often find ourselves in a state of flux, feeling as though we are perpetually shitting off of a ladder. The ladder in this instance represents the constructs of society that we often find ourselves struggling against, and the act of shitting suggests the ways in which we try to break free from these expectations and rules. Ultimately, the work is a commentary on the human condition and the way in which we all must strive to find our own balance in a world that is constantly trying to pull us down.
