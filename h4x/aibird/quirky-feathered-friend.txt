"Odd Goosr" is a satirical commentary on society's desire to categorize and label everything, even the seemingly insignificant. Through the use of a hybrid creature, half-goose and half-ostrich, I challenge the audience to question the absurdity of our obsession with labeling and fitting things into neat boxes. The grotesque and unconventional nature of the "odd goosr" serves as a thought-provoking and humorous symbol for the rigidity of our societal norms.
