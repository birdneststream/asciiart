'Wacky Races' is a satirical exploration of the absurdity and competitiveness inherent in the human desire for entertainment. Through the lens of a whimsical and exaggerated race, each character represents a different societal archetype, highlighting the various ways in which we are all racing towards an illusory finish line. With intricate visual references and commentary on our collective societal constructs, 'Wacky Races' challenges the viewer to question the true purpose and value of our constant pursuit of winning.
