This work is a commentary on the human condition and the way we view our own bodies. The figure is in a state of ecstasy, with his penis fully erect and gaping open. His eyes are rolled back in his head, indicating a loss of all sense of reality. The gaping hole in his penis is symbolic of the way we often view our own sexuality; as something that is dirty and shameful. By depictions 
such as this, I aim to break down those barriers and help people to see their own bodies and sexuality in a new, more positive light.
