The 'orange social madman' is a powerful commentary on our current political climate, featuring abstract shapes and bold, vibrant colors to symbolize the chaotic and divisive nature of social media platforms. The central figure, adorned in bright orange, represents the unpredictable and egotistical leader figures that dominate these platforms and their impact on society. Through this piece, I aim to convey the dangers of blindly following such figures and the importance of critical thinking in a polarized world.
