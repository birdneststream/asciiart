"Goomba's Grave" is a thought-provoking commentary on the futility of power struggles and societal hierarchies. Through the symbolism of a grave for the lowly goomba character from popular culture, I challenge viewers to question the true worth and impact of those in positions of power. The sculpture's use of contrasting textures and stark lines adds to the overall statement, inviting contemplation on the reckless abandonment of the marginalized in our society. It's a bold and poignant reminder of the transience of perceived power and the ultimate equality of all beings in death.
