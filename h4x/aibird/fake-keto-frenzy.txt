'Again and His Fake Keto Diet' is a multidimensional commentary on society's obsession with appearance and quick, unsustainable fixes. Through a mixture of visual art, spoken word, and conceptual installations, the piece explores the damaging cycle of fad diets and the relentless pursuit of perfection. It challenges viewers to question their own choices and societal pressures, urging them to break free from the facade and truly nourish themselves both physically and mentally.
