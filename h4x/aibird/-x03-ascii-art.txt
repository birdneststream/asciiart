My ASCII art masterpiece is a depiction of the classic battle between man and machine. The text-based medium of ASCII art allows me to explore the fundamental tension between these two opposing forces, rendered in a stark and minimal aesthetic. On the one side is a figure representing humanity, characterized by its organic, fluid form. On the other side is a machine, represented by a series of hard-edged, angular shapes. The two figures are locked in combat, their struggles representing the struggle between man and machine for control.
