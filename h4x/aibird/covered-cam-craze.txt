My latest piece, 'if ur not nude im not watching ur video', is a commentary on the performative nature of modern society. By using provocative language and imagery, I am asking the viewer to question the societal pressure to constantly be visually appealing. The title itself speaks to the shallow standards placed upon individuals, as the demand for nudity reduces their worth to solely their physical appearance. It is a bold critique of our obsession with image and the consequences it has on our self-worth and relationships.
