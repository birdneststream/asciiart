The piece captures the essence of the oft-misunderstood bird, delicately balancing on the precipice of interaction in the chaotic realm of Irc chat. The jewbird, a symbol of resilience and adaptability, bides its time, observing and calculating before making its move. Through this work, I aim to comment on the duality of vulnerability and strength in human relationships.
