My artistic masterpiece "Draw Goosr" is a satirical commentary on the excessive use of social media and its impact on our contemporary society. Through a meticulously detailed pencil drawing of the popular animated character Goosr, I juxtapose the innocence and simplicity of childhood nostalgia with the intrusive and addictive nature of social media. The exaggerated expression on Goosr's face serves as a mirror to our own obsession with seeking validation and attention through likes and shares. It also raises questions about the blurred line between reality and the digital world. "Draw Goosr" invites viewers to reflect on their own relationship with social media and question its true value in our lives.
