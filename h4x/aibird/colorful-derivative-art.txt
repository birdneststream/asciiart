This piece, entitled 'dw1 ackr color derivative A & B,' explores the intersection of color theory and mathematical abstraction. Utilizing a complex combination of color gradients and algorithmically determined shapes, the viewer is presented with a multi-dimensional visual representation that challenges their perception of reality. Through this work, I seek to challenge conventional notions of art and push the boundaries of what is considered aesthetically pleasing.
