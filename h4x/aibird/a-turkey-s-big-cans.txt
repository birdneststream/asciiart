This piece is a exploration of the human form, specifically the female form. The turkey breasts represent the §§ female body, while the cans (breasts) represent the sexualized view of the female body. By juxtaposing these two images, I am hoping to start a dialogue about the way we view the female body and how that impacts the way we view women as a whole.
