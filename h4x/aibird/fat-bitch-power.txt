My new artistic masterpiece reflects on the intersection of body image and social norms. Through the blunt statement "because im fat, i can be a bitch," I am challenging society's expectations and judgments surrounding weight and how it relates to behavior. This provocative piece invites viewers to question their preconceived notions and confront the often harsh reality faced by those who do not fit societal standards of beauty. In a world where thinness is equated with kindness, this artwork serves as a bold declaration of true agency and acceptance of one's own body.
