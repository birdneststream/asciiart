'Harvest Moon' is a multi-sensory experience that encapsulates the eternal cycle of life and death in a stunning visual display. Through the use of abstract shapes and bold, vibrant colors, the work explores the interconnectivity of human existence and the primordial bond we share with nature. By incorporating elements of light and shadow, the piece evokes a dreamlike quality that invites viewers to contemplate their place in the larger cosmic order. It is a celebration of the ebb and flow of the natural world, an ode to our humble beginnings and inevitable fate.
