My latest piece delves into the world of truck drivers and their, shall we say, virility. Through a combination of multimedia elements, I explore the idea that these gruff and rugged individuals possess a certain potency, specifically in their semen. It is a bold statement on the relationship between masculinity and fertility, challenging traditional notions of potency and changing cultural norms. With a touch of satire, the piece invites the viewer to question societal expectations and embrace a more nuanced understanding of masculinity.
