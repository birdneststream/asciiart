After long hours spent pleasuring others, the human body requires a period of rest and rejuvenation. My latest work, "after 11 hours of sucking dick you need 10 hours rest," captures this delicate balance between work and self-care. Through simplistic yet provocative imagery, I examine the sacrifices and physical demands of modern labor and the necessity of self-care in a capitalist society. This piece challenges societal norms and urges us to prioritize our own well-being in the face of constant demands to perform.
