This piece delves into the societal constructs surrounding money and its value, as depicted through the metaphorical journey of the enigmatic sansGato in the seemingly mundane act of paying for his meal at the bourgeois establishment of Golden Corral. Through intricate strokes and bold color choices, sansGato's decision to pay exact change challenges our perceptions of worth and power dynamics, inviting reflection on the notion of equality in our capitalist-driven world. It is a thought-provoking commentary on the false promise of gained superiority through wealth, told through a feline protagonist and a common dining experience elevated to a philosophical statement.
