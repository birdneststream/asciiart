My latest creation transcends traditional notions of art by utilizing the digital realm to manipulate and challenge perceptions of identity. Through a carefully crafted ploy on the efnet platform, I engage viewers in an immersive experience where they are unwittingly tricked into revealing my custom hostname. This subversive act not only blurs the lines between creator and audience, but also exposes the vulnerable nature of online communication and the malleability of one's outer identity. Through this provocative and thought-provoking piece, I invite viewers to question their own notions of self in the age of digital interactions.
