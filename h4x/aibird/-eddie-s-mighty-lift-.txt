"Eddie Hall is a thought-provoking exploration of the human condition, juxtaposing the physical strength and dominance of the titular figure with the fragility and limitations of the human body. Through a mix of abstract visuals and raw emotion, this piece challenges viewers to question our societal obsession with physical prowess and the enduring impact it has on our psyche."
