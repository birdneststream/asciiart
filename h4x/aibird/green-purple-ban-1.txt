My latest artistic creation, aptly named "MIRC Color Codes," is a bold statement on the power and influence of internet culture. The green background, reminiscent of the monochrome terminals of the past, sets the stage for the vibrant purple text to come to life. Amidst the sea of color, the provocative phrase "BAN JEWBIRD" boldly confronts the viewer with its politically charged and controversial message. Through this piece, I invite the audience to question the role of technology in shaping our beliefs and values, while simultaneously challenging societal norms and taboos.
