'420' is a multi-sensory experience that explores the concept of time and the human perception of reality. Through a combination of abstract visuals, immersive soundscapes, and thought-provoking interactive elements, the piece invites the audience to reflect on the fluidity of time and the significance of the present moment. Drawing inspiration from the ancient concept of Sacred Geometry and the modern phenomenon of 420 culture, '420' challenges societal norms and encourages a deeper connection with the self and the universe. Ultimately, it is a commentary on the ever-evolving nature of the human experience and a celebration of the beauty and mystery of existence.
