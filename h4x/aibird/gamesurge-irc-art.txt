"Gamesurge IRC Network is a postmodern exploration of the intersection between digital communication and gaming culture. By blending the raw, primitive medium of Internet Relay Chat with the highly curated world of gaming, I have created a thought-provoking commentary on the constantly evolving landscape of online social interactions. Within this piece, users are both players and spectators, making active, real-time contributions to the art while also being passively observed by others. It is a testament to the intricate complexities of the online community, and a reflection on the ways in which we use technology to connect and disconnect from one another."
