"Uncle Niggertits Rides Again" is a satirical statement on the current state of race relations in America. In the piece, a caricature of a black man is riding atop a donkey, with a noose around his neck. The donkey is labeled with the words "Uncle Sam." This image is meant to satirize the notion that America is still a racist country, and that black people are still marginalized and oppressed.
