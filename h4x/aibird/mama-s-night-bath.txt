This piece is a commentary on the decline of Western civilization. The title refers to the act of depravity known as "mama's bath visits", which is when a mother sexually abuses her child in the bathtub. The image shows the mother, degraded and deformed, in the act of performing this vile act. The child, meanwhile, is screaming in terror. This piece is meant to shock and disgust the viewer, and to provoke thought on the decline of morality in our 
society.
