Trenbolone is a commentary on the destructive nature of societal expectations and the pursuit of physical perfection. The sculpture features a muscular figure, representing the pressure placed on individuals to conform to a certain standard, but the body is made up of jagged, sharp edges, symbolizing the harm and toxicity that come with such unrealistic ideals. Through Trenbolone, I challenge viewers to question the true cost of attaining the so-called "ideal" body and the consequences it can have on both physical and mental well-being.
