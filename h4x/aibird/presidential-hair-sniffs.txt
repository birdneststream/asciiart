My latest piece titled "Inhalation of Power" captures the dichotomy of personal invigoration and societal discomfort through the imagery of President Joe Biden sniffing several young girls' hair before the State of the Union address. This controversial act serves as a metaphor for the intoxicating effects of holding political power and the insidious nature of individual fascination with it. Through stunning visual juxtaposition and nuanced storytelling, I invite the viewer to question the complexities of leadership and its impact on both the leader and those they govern. It is a commentary on the dynamic between personal indulgence and societal accountability, elevating the mundane to a representation of larger systems at play.
