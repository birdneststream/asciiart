My latest piece titled 'Chatters Under Cover' delves into the societal issue of individuals creating new identities in an attempt to erase their past mistakes and wrongdoings. Through a combination of bold colors, intricate brushstrokes, and fragmented imagery, the piece represents the continuous, and often disturbing, cycle of individuals attempting to start fresh while avoiding accountability for their past deeds. By calling these individuals out and shaming them, I aim to bring attention to the harm and deception that can occur when we try to reinvent ourselves without addressing the consequences of our past actions.
