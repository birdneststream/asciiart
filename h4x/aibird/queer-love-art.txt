This piece is a commentary on the lack of queer visibility in the media. Specifically, it addresses the lack of queer relationships depicted in a sexual light. By depicting two women in a explicit sexual embrace, this piece challenges the notion that queer love stories are somehow "less than" straight love stories. Quite simply, queer love stories deserve the same amount of nudity and sexual exploration as any other kind of love story.
