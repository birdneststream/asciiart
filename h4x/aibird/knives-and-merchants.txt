In this work, I explore the stark contrast between the commodification of violence and the haggling that takes place between Knifey, the knife-wielding assailant, and the merchant who tries to sell him a weapon. Through the juxtaposition of these two characters, I aimed to raise questions about the role of capitalism in objectifying and de-humanizing people.
