"Ramones is a postmodern commentary on consumerism and mass culture, exploring the interplay between manufactured celebrity and self-identity. The intricate mixed media installation juxtaposes iconic elements of pop culture with subversive motifs, inviting the viewer to question the commodification of individualism in modern society. Through a satirical lens, Ramones challenges conventional notions of fame, exposing the absurdity of our obsession with manufactured personas. It is a thought-provoking and visually-stunning piece that challenges the viewer to question their own perceptions of self and society."
