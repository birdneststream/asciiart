My new artwork, "let your hormones off the hook," is a postmodern exploration of the societal implications of hormone imbalances. By deconstructing the traditional artistic interpretation of the human form, I seek to provoke thought about the ways in which our culture objectifies and fetishizes the female body. My work is a challenge to the traditional patriarchal values that continue to pervade our society, and an attempt to create a more inclusive and empowering narrative for women.
