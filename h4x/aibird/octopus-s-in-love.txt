This new piece is a commentary on the dark side of humanity, specifically the commercialization of sex. The title refers to the 69 octopi that were rescued from a brothel in Thailand where they had been trained to provide sexual services to customers. The octopi are a symbol of the victims of this dark trade, who are often exploited and treated as objects instead of sentient beings. The work is meant to provoke thought and conversation about this controversial issue.
