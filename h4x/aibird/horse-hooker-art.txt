This work is a comment on themodal horse-headed lyre player in the Louvre. The figure is basedon an Etruscan tomb painting from Tarquinia (c. 470 BC), which itimitates in posture and proportions. However, in my work, the horsehas been replaced with a hooker, thus commenting on the frivolity and emptiness of the bourgeoisie artistic elite.
