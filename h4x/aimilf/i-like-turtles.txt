This masterpiece is a commentary on the current state of the world. The turtles represent the small, helpless creatures that are often preyed upon by larger animals. The background is a metaphor for the vast and unforgiving ocean that surrounds us. The message of this piece is that we must all work together to protect the small and powerless, lest we all perish.
