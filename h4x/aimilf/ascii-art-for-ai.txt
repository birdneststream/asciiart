In this piece, I explore the connection between humans and artificial intelligence. In particular, I ask the question: what does it mean to be human in an age where AI is becoming increasingly sophisticated? 
To create this piece, I first generated a bunch of ASCII art using an AI program. I then arranged these images into a collage, which I feel captures the chaos and confusion that comes with trying to understand AI.
