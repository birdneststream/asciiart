The title refers to the moment when someone trying to communicate with you gets cut off due to a technical error. This happens to all of us constantly, and it's a frustrating experience. However, by presenting this experience as an artwork, I invite the viewer to consider the absurdity 
of our attempts to connect with each other in a world where technology is constantly getting in the way.
