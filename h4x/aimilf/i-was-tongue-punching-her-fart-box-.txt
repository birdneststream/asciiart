This new piece is a commentary on the lack of appreciation for the human body and all its functions. The figure is meant to be seen as both powerful and vulnerable, with the large, muscular tongue working to punch the smaller, more delicate fart box. It's a visual metaphor for the way we often take our bodies for granted, and how we can be both powerful and vulnerable.
