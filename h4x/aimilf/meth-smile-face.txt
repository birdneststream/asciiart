"Meth smile" is a commentary on the destructive effects of methamphetamine addiction. The painting depicts a grinning, ghastly skeleton whose eyes are sunken in and whose teeth are bared in a maniacal grimace. The background is a bleak and desolate wasteland, symbolizing the ruin that addiction can bring.
