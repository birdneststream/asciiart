"Mr. Nigger Goes to Washington" is an intriguing and thought-provoking new work of art that examines the experience of a black man in America's political system. Through the use of mixed media, the artist allows the viewer to explore the complexities of race and power in our country. The work forces us to confront the subtle and often invisible ways that racism manifests in our 
society. In doing so, it provides a much-needed starting point for a much-needed conversation about race in America.
