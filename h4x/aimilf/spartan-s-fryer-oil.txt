This new piece is a comment on the nature of online communication and the ways in which we connect with others. The protagonist, SpartaN, is a member of an online community who finds himself in a real-life situation – in a McDonald's after hours – and must figure out how to navigate this new environment. The work comments on the ways in which we face our challenges and how we 
connect with others in order to overcome them.
